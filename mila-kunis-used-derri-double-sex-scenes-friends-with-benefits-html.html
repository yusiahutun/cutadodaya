Mila Kunis 'used a derrire double in sex scenes' for Friends With Benefits

'I used a derrière double in sex scenes,' reveals Friends With Benefits star Mila Kunis

Mila Kunis has revealed she used a body double during her sex scenes in Friends With Benefits.

The 27-year-old was prepared to show 'side-boob' in the romantic comedy with Justin Timberlake, but stopped short of revealing her naked behind.

The pair play two friends who embark on a purely physical relationship in new movie.

Cheeky replacement: Mila Kunis, at the Friends with Benefits New York premiere earlier this week, has revealed she used a body double in some scenes

In an interview with Ryan Seacrest his Kiss FM radio show, she discussed the process of casting her look alike.

'These young ladies came in and they were in a casting room with the director and the casting director and my makeup artists,' she recalled.

'These lovely women had to show us their derrière and we chose the one that resembled my body the most without looking like it is fake.'

Naked ambition: The actress gets up close and personal with Justin Timberlake in the romantic comedy

Explaining her decision, she said: 'I showed side boob and I figured I can't just give everything all away at once. I gotta let it all out in little pieces here and there.'

Meanwhile, her co-star Justin has joked that he initially found the idea of working with a naked Mila 'gross'.

He  teased that the required stripping off was not a selling point.

He told MTV News: 'I wasn't begging [to be naked].

'When I found out Mila was going to be naked, I was really sort of like, 'Ewww, gross', ' he joked.

'But I guess if that's what it takes to make the movie, then I'll do it.'

Playing along, Kunis said: '[You are] such a hard worker. You're committed.' 

Friends With Benefits is released in the U.S. July 22 and the U.K. September 9.
