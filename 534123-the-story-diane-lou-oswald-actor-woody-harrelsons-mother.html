Interesting facts about Diane Lou Oswald?

Who is Woody Harrelson's mom? Diane Lou Oswald. And what are some unknown facts about her? Here are details about Diane Oswald:

1. Her ex-husband was a contract killer

In 1959, Diane married Charles Harrelson. Charles worked as an encyclopaedia salesman in California and was a professional gambler. The couple welcomed three children until 1964 when he left his matrimonial home. During their marriage, Harrelson got involved in crime.

In 1960, he was convicted of armed robbery. He later admitted that he had been involved in dozens of murders beginning in the early 1960s. In subsequent years, Diane's ex-husband became a contract killer. In 1968, Charles was tried for the 1968 murder of Alan Harry Berg. Fortunately, a jury in Angleton acquitted him on 22 September 1970.

In 1968, a court tried Charles Harrelson for the 1968 murder-for-hire killing of Sam Degelia Jr., a resident of Hearne, Texas. Harrelson received $2,000 for the murder of Degelia, a grain dealer and father of four killed in McAllen, Texas. His first trial ended with a deadlocked jury.

The court found him guilty of being an accomplice to the murder and sentenced him to seven years probation. But the same court retried Harrelson in 1973, convicted him, and sentenced him to 15 years. The American convict was later released from prison in 1978 for good behaviour.

After his release from prison, the police implicated Harrelson and his then-wife, Jo Ann, in another murder of US district judge John H. Wood Jr. The court later convicted him of killing Judge Wood because a drug dealer had hired him. It then sentenced him to two life terms.

In 2003, a witness to the murder claimed that Harrelson never shot the judge. Then, in September 1980, Charles surrendered to police after a six-hour standoff in which he was reportedly high on coke. During the standoff, he threatened to state that he had killed Judge Wood and President John F. Kennedy. However, he later recanted the story.

2. Diane Lou Oswald’s children are famous actors

Diane and Charles Harrelson welcomed three children, Brett Harrelson, Woody Harrelson, and Jordan Harrelson. Woody Harrelson was born Woodrow Tracy Harrelson on 23 July 1961 in Texas, United States.

He is an actor known for his roles in The People vs. Larry Flynt. The Messenger, Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri, The Highwaymen, and for playing Haymitch Abernathy in The Hunger Games film series.

Brett Harrelson, born on 4th June 1963, is also an American actor. Her youngest son, Jordan Harrelson, is known for his roles in Between the Stars, Well, and Broken Promise.

3. She raised her children alone

Diane separated from her husband, Charles Harrelson, in 1964. The two were only married for five years. According to Woody Harrelson, his father disappeared from the family's home in Houston in 1968.

As a result, he left his mother to raise him and his two siblings. They lost track of him until 1981, when Charles was arrested and later convicted of the murder of Judge Wood.

Even after several convictions, Diane Lou never turned his children against their father. Woody admits that he visited his father regularly in federal prison despite harbouring mixed feelings about him.

4. Diane Lou is not related to Lee Harvey Oswald

At the time the authorities were arresting Charles Harrelson for killing Judge Wood, Harrelson admitted he had assassinated the 39th President of the USA, John F. Kennedy. However, the person who assassinated the president was Lee Harvey Oswald, a US Marine veteran.

Is Diane Oswald related to Lee Harvey Oswald? While this is a question many people have asked themselves, the two are not related. Their parents are different, as in Diane Oswald's family tree.

Diane Lou Oswald’s parents are Kenneth Oswald and Mary Lou Oswald. On the other hand, Lee Harvey Oswald’s parents were MetLife worker Robert Edward Lee Oswald Sr. (1896–1939) and legal clerk Marguerite Frances Claverie (1907–1981).

5. She has three grandchildren

Diane Lou Oswald’s family tree begins with her grandparents, Dutch and Izora Oswald. Diane's parents are Kenneth Oswald and Mary Lou Oswald.

Her partner was Charles Harrelson, with whom they welcomed three children: Brett Harrelson, Woody Harrelson, and Jordan Harrelson. Diane Lou Oswald’s grandkids are Deni Harrelson, Zoe Harrelson, and Makani Harrelson.
