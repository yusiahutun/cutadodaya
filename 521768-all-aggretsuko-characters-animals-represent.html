All Aggretsuko characters and the animals they represent

You might be familiar with the Japanese animation series Aggretsuko if you are an animation fan. The series uses wild animal characters as its cast, making for a visually appealing and entertaining concept. But if you've watched the show, you might wonder which animals the various Aggretsuko characters are.

Aggretsuko is an animation series created by Yeti for Saniro, a Japanese entertainment company. Saniro designs, licenses, and produces products, like Hello Kitty, focusing on the kawaii or cute segment of Japanese popular culture. Aggretsuko aired as a series of animated shorts on Japan's TBS Television in 2016. Netflix released an anime series adaptation worldwide in 2018.

Aggretsuko characters

If you have been privileged to watch Aggretsuko, you might have noticed the various animal characters that make the cast. The show is based on modern-day Japanese culture and features animal characters from different regions of the world.

So, which animals do the characters represent? According to the Animal Character Database, here are all Aggretsuko characters and the animals they represent.

1. Retsuko – Red panda

Retsuko, the show's main character, is a red panda. She is among the top female characters in Aggretsuko and has black eyes and orange fur. Retsuko is a 25-year-old frustrated by the direction her life and career is heading.

She takes her frustration out at a karaoke bar, singing death metal daily. The other red panda characters include Retsuko's boyfriend in season 1, Resasuko, and her mother.

According to WWF, the red panda is a small mammal, more significant than a cat, native to the eastern Himalayas and southwest China.

2. Director Ton – Duroc pig

Director Ton is one of the villains in Aggretsuko. He is a detestable, misogynistic, patronising and crude boss who doesn't appreciate anything his subordinates do. Ton is a Duroc pig, an older breed of domestic pig, due to his distinctive brown-reddish hair.

3. Fenneko – Fennec fox

Fenneko is Retsuko's co-worker and closest friend in the office. She is a fennec fox, a small crepuscular fox native to the deserts of North Africa, with distinctive huge ears. Fenneko is highly perceptive and insightful and can deduce people's mental states through scrutinising their social media.

4. Haida – Spotted hyena

Haida is among the top male Aggretsuko characters appearing in all seasons. Haida is a 27-year-old mild-mannered spotted hyena, one of Retsuko's co-workers. He is an avid punk rock fan and plays bass guitar.

In the early seasons of Aggretsuko, Haida has a crush on Restuko and tries vainly to win her heart. He eventually wins it, becoming Retsuko's boyfriend later in the series.

According to Nat Geo Kids, the spotted hyena, also known as the laughing hyena, is a species native to sub-Saharan Africa.

5. Director Gori – Gorrilla

Gori is a western lowland gorilla working as the marketing director at Retsuko's company. She is highly excitable and takes great interest in Retsuko bonding with her after doing yoga and, eventually, karaoke with her.

According to the National Zoo, the western lowland gorilla is native to central Africa's forest and lowland swampland.

6. Ms. Washimi – Secretary bird

Ms. Washimi is a 40-year-old secretary bird working as the secretary to the company's CEO. She is wise and strong-willed and helps Retsuko with advice on her life. The secretary bird is a large, mostly terrestrial bird of prey endemic to Africa's open grasslands and savanna of the sub-Saharan region.

7. Tsunoda – Thomson's gazelle

Tsunoda is a Thomson's gazelle who frequently butters up to director Ton and her superiors to remain favourable and lighten her workload. Her shameless attitude earns her the ire of her co-workers. But despite her personality, she is self-aware and more genuine than people perceive her.

Per the African Wildlife Foundation, the Thomson's gazelle is an animal native to the savannas of East Africa, particularly the Serengeti in Kenya and Tanzania.

8. Komiya – Meerkat

Komiya is Director Ton's right-hand subordinate, and like Tsunoda, he butters up to his superior not for self-gratification but admiration for his supervisor, Ton. Komiya is a meerkat, a small mongoose from southern Africa with a broad head, large eyes, a pointed snout, and long legs. The animal also has a thin tapering tail and a brindled coat pattern.

9. Tsubone – Komodo dragon

Tsubone is a Komodo dragon, also known as the Komodo monitor, a member of the monitor lizard family Varanidae. Tsubone is senior to Retsuko in the accounting department and frequently uses her seniority to push extra work to Retsuko.

10. Kabae – Hippopotamus

Kabae is one of Retsuko's co-workers. She is a middle-aged hippopotamus renowned for spreading gossip in the office. Kabae is excited by office buzz but claims she never spreads anything malicious.

11. Anai – Japanese badger

Anai is a Japanese badger and a new hire in the trading firm's accounting department, fresh from college (season 2). Though happy and eager to join the trading firm, he doesn't take criticism lightly. The Japanese badger is a species of the family Mustelidae, the weasels and their kin and is endemic to Japan.

12. Tadano – Donkey

Tadano is a character from Aggretsuko season 2. He is a donkey initially shown as a lazy and jobless bum. However, Tadano is incredibly wealthy and intelligent and founded an AI company quickly rising in value.

13. Hyodo – Leopard

Hyodo is a leopard that works as a window washer at the trading firm's building. He works as a window washer daily and a manager for the idol group OTM Girls at night. Hyodo meets Retsuko after she accidentally crashes into his van. Retsuko takes up a second job to repay the debt.

14. Manaka – Chinchillas

Manaka is the lead singer of the girl group OTM Girls. She is a chinchilla, a crepuscular rodent species more giant and robust than ground squirrels. Chinchillas are native to the Andes mountains in South America. Manaka is confident and bold, often behaves like a diva, and has an aggressive persona.

15. Inui – Borzoi dog

Inui is a character introduced in season three. She is a Borzoi dog, or Russian Hunting Sighthound is a Russian breed of hunting dog of sighthound type. Inui works in the General Affairs department at the trading firm and is an avid punk rock fan.

16. Himuro – Saluki dog

Himuro joins the show in season four as the External Director of the trading firm. He gets promoted to CEO after the hospitalisation of the original CEO. Himuro is a Saluki dog, a standardised breed developed from sighthounds once used by nomadic tribes to run down game animals.

17. Shikabane – Skunk

Shikabane is a 21-year-old purple skunk from Aggretsuko season five. She lives in an internet cafe, working freelance jobs to sustain herself.
