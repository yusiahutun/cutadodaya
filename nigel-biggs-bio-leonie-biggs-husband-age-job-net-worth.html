Meet Nigel Biggs, Dancing Queens Star Leonie Biggs Husband

Dancing Queens star Leonie Biggs and her husband Nigel Biggs met each other in 2002. In the summer of the year, the two drifters were off in different directions to see the world. After eight years of meeting each other, Nigel and Leonie exchanged their wedding vows in January 2010. On their 12th anniversary in January 2022, Leonie posted a photo that she calls a “treasure” because it was the first photo taken of them (using a disposable camera, so before the digital age).

It was also the first gift a penny-less Nigel ever gave to her. She wrote in the caption, “It was so emotional as it was his ‘forget me not’ just prior to us parting ways to start jobs in different states (or so we thought). The married couple shares one daughter named Charlotte. Born in the fall of 2015, Charlotte is 7 years old.

Apart from dancing, Leonie is a model, and martial artist, and works in finance. According to her experience section, Leonie started working as an institutional trader at Patersons’ Securities in 1999. She rose to the position of senior account manager at HOSTPLUS after four years of employment there. Her subsequent position was as an IBD Associate at Goldman Sachs, but after a year and a half there, she changed employers and was appointed head of UK operations at Alternative Investment Management Products (UK) Limited.

She began working at PwC LLP in 2014 as a Management Consultant – Asset Management. She is still employed there, according to her LinkedIn profile, and she is also a lifestyle model and an entrepreneur philanthropist.
