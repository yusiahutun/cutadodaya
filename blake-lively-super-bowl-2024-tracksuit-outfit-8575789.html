See Blake Lively's Sporty Super Bowl 2024 Outfit

There's no denying Super Bowl LVIII is about more than just the Kansas City Chiefs facing off against the San Francisco 49ers. Sure, sports fans everywhere are tuning in to see who wins the Big Game, but there's also a whole contingent of Swifties getting into football for a chance at a glance of their favorite pop star and her celebrity crew.

On Sunday, February 11, Blake Lively arrived at Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas alongside Taylor Swift and Ice Spice. Swift, who's attending the game to support her boyfriend and Kansas City Chiefs tight end, Travis Kelce, brought her friends along with her, and their outfits did not disappoint.

While Swift went with head-to-toe black and sparkle, Lively showed team spirit by wearing a two-piece Adidas tracksuit in Kansas City red. She paired the matching jacket and pants with a ribbed white cropped tank and chunky gold jewelry. She also opted for big '90s curls swept to the side for maximum volume. The overall effect is very Sporty Spice meets supermodel.

Of course, this isn't the first time Lively has accompanied Taylor Swift to a football game. Back in October, the actress, who's slated to star in the highly-anticipated film adaption of Colleen Hoover's It Ends With Us, joined Swift; her husband, Ryan Reynolds; and a group of other celebs to cheer on Travis Kelce at MetLife Stadium in New Jersey when the Chiefs played the New York Jets.
