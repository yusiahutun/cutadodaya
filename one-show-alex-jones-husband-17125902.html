Alex Jones' boob leaked live on The One Show

Alex Jones' baby boy Edward Alun Burrell Thomson, or Teddy for short, arrived on January 22 2017.

The One Show host returned to work just three months later after Angela Scanlon took over while she was on maternity leave.

However, the 41-year-old has now admitted that she raced back to work a little too soon.

The Strictly Come Dancing 2011 bombshell suffered an unfortunate blunder when her boob leaked live on air.

The move meant she had to switch to formula milk to prevent any more mishaps on the BBC programme.

Alex was breastfeeding her son at the time and would express milk at work.

"My milk was in the fridge with all the food and sandwiches that were being given to the guests! I was like: 'This is not working, this is a nightmare,'" she revealed on the Happy Mum Happy Baby Podcast.

"I'd sit on the sofa and the titles would start playing and I'd feel this stain start coming through the dress. There was nothing I could do about it.

"It destroyed my confidence in terms of trying to feed and be back at work. By four months I had to [switch] to formula completely.

"Like a lot of people I put a lot of pressure on myself, but Teddy thrived just as well on formula as he did on breast milk."

Alex – born Charlotte Alexandra Jones – said she felt pressure to race back to the programme because she's the main breadwinner in the family.

The Welsh TV presenter has been hosting The One Show since 2010 alongside Matt Baker.

The news chat show – which first aired in 2006 – has previously been fronted by Christine Lampard, Myleene Klass and Nadia Sawalha.

The One Show airs weeknights at 7pm on BBC1.
