Chinese construction companies in Kenya

Chinese construction companies in Kenya are more of a threat to the Kenyan construction companies and contractors. With Chinese companies using modernized equipment, many of the small scale companies in Kenya have fallen prey. This in turn has lead to loss of construction jobs in Kenya, and many have sunk in debts.
