Ernestine Sclafani Bio, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Ernestine Sclafani is a New York-born public relations expert and the known celebrity wife of the famous television personality, Skip Bayless. She is a media relations guru and began to attract media attention after her connection with her recent husband Skip Bayless. Ernestine is not active on social media in any form and appeared with her husband on the FS1 show on a couple of occasions last year.

Early Life

Ernestine Sclafani was born on 3rd August 1962, in Long Island, New York, United States. Her mother’s name is Evelyn Sclafani, who died in August 2013 due to pancreatic cancer. Her father’s name is not known. She shared her childhood with her one brother and one sister and both of her siblings are doctors. Her brother is a doctor in Manhattan, New York, and her sister is a breast surgeon in Ohio. Ernestine went to Suffolk County Community College in Selden, New York, and additionally earned another degree from the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City.

Ernestine Sclafani Career

Sclafani is a known American media relation and public relations specialist. She has worked in public relationships with many celebrities and high-profile clients including celebrities, reality television stars, famous chefs, and authors. She has worked in the line of handling celebrity media tours as well as satellite media tours for social media influencers. In addition, she is a public relations expert and handles professional lines including fashion, lifestyle, marketing, and event planning. Before she began her own public relations company in 2018, Ernestine was working as the Vice President for Consumer Media Relations at Weber Shandwick. During that time, she led the product launch for brands and clients like Oreo, Dunkin Donuts, Pedigree, Unilever, Harley Davidson, Motorola, etc.
