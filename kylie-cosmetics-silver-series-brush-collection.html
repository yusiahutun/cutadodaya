Kylie Cosmetics Silver Series Brush Collection

Kylie Jenner announced this week that bullet lipsticks, liquid concealers, and a mobile makeup van will be joining the Kylie Cosmetics family this holiday season. Her last surprise-drop is a collection of 16 "luxury" makeup brushes launching on December 13. The word luxury is probably in the name because the collection will cost you a whopping $360.

Fans took to Instagram to praise the sleek silver design of the brushes, however, they were quick to call-out the exorbitant price tag.

The limited edition full set—which includes all 16 brushes in a metallic soft case—costs $360. If you were to buy each brush individually, the total would come to $378.
