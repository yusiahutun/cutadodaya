Trey Kennedy-Bio

Trey Kennedy was born on 17th November 1992 in Oklahoma, the United States. Similarly, Kennedy is American as per nationality and as far as his ethnicity is concerned, he is White. Moreover, his birth sign is Scorpio which defines his personality to be passionate and intelligent.

Kennedy’s parents raised him in Edmond and Stillwater, Oklahoma along with his younger sister, Kaci Lynn. Further, Kennedy started his career in Vine by posting a video on 18th February 2013 and became consistent in posting videos. He grabbed millions of fans on his Vine but the app eventually shut down.

Similarly, in 2015, Kennedy started his YouTube career by creating a self-titled YouTube channel. On 18th December 2015, he posted a cover video, ”Tennessee Whiskey (Cover)” which grabbed over 198k followers. He then posts many comedy and singing videos. As of 2018, his channel has over 93k subscribers and thousands of views.
