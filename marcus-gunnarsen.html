Biography

They won the 11th season of Melodi Grand Prix Junior and put out a debut album called Hei in February of 2015. They were named Artists of the Year at the 2017 Norwegian Grammys. He and Martinus went on tour with Jason Derulo in 2018. He is close friends with Lisa and Lena Mantler.
