Kim Kardashian Sheer Jumpsuit - Kim Kardashian See Through Jumpsuit with Shorts

How many sheer pieces can Kim Kardashian wear in a week? Following last Friday's translucent dress and last night's sheer Balmain skirt comes the reality star's latest skin-baring look: a mesh jumpsuit.

As if the see-through onepiece wasn't revealing enough, it also included a jagged plunging neckline that dropped to her stomach, and slits on each shoulder that went down to her underarms.

DPXimages

Like her recent nipple-freeing ensembles, this one did not include a bra, but it did include some iteration of underwear bottoms. Kim covered up with sleek black biker shorts, but that's all.

She applied the same minimalist mentality when it came to accessories. The star opted for clear heels sandals and didn't even carry a purse—she just kept her bare phone in her hand. For a social media-savvy celeb with millions of followers and counting, what else does she need?
