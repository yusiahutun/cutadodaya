Travis Kelce avoids embarrassment after forgetting underwear on Andrew Santino's podcast

After hearing about Jason Kelce’s underwear antics last week, this time it’s Travis’ turn to divulge, about the time he ‘forgot his underwear’ on Andrew Santino’s pod. We don’t think these two know what TMI means…

Every Wednesday Jason and Travis Kelce entertain us with their weekly on their goings-on their New Heights podcast. From their relationships, (yes that does include Taylor Swift), to their careers, and their underwear choices the brothers don’t hold back. Although they do happen to forget to wear underwear sometimes…

Travis Kelce ‘forgot his underwear’ on Andrew Santino’s podcast

In their segment, ‘No Dumb Questions,’ one viewer got straight to the point, and asked guest Andrew Santino, “Did Trav really forget his undergarments four years ago when he came on your podcast?”

Unfortunately, the answer was yes, the Hollywood star revealed. Four years ago, Travis appeared as a guest on his Whiskey Ginger podcast. Everything went well and all was filmed until the editor started doing his job.

“My editor calls me, and he’s like, ‘bro, I don’t know how to tell you this, but Travis was falling out of his shorts’,” Santino revealed as the Chiefs star burst into laughter.

“I was like ‘What do you mean falling out of his shorts?'” Adam recalled asking his podcast editor, who responded “‘His boys were sliding out.'” Awkward…

Instead of going through the effort to reshoot, the Ricky Stanicky star had the clever idea of covering the ‘inappropriate areas’ with a Kansas City Chiefs logo whenever they were visible.

Ending on a joke the movie star said they were “going to sell that footage for a lot of money someday.”

“No wonder you wear underwear now,” brother Jason Kelce exclaimed. Luckily, the former Philadelphia Eagles star has never had that problem, despite admitting he only wears underwear when working out. Yep, the rest of the time he goes commando.

Travis Kelce and Taylor are reportedly planning a family vacay – so he better pack his underwear

It’s been reported that the celebrity couple are taking their families on an ‘epic European trip’ as the Bad Blood singer gears up the European segment of her Eras Tour next month.

An insider tells the US Sun that Ed and Donna Kelce, as well as Taylor’s parents, and Jason will be involved in the trip, popping in here and there.

“The plan is for the families to link up at the beginning of May for the four-show run in France and will stay until May 30,” the source claims.

“Once the four dates in Paris are over, they are thinking of heading for some culinary and visual delights in Tuscany, before setting off for Sweden where four gigs are planned at Stockholm’s Friends Arena.”

The NFL star is planning to stay on as the tour heads back to France and the United Kingdom, including eight dates at Wembley Arena, the source reveals, while the rest of the family heads back to the States.

“They have been talking about it for a while now and they all are so excited for this Euro trip,” the insider added. “It’s a great time and opportunity to get to know each other’s families more.”
