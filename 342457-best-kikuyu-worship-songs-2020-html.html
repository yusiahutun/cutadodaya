Best Kikuyu worship songs 2020

It is one thing to master the art of doing something and earning a living out of it, and it’s another thing to touch the hearts of men with uplifting music. A majority of the Kenyan musicians have been on a mission to achieve either of the above quests. This hasn't been different for the Kikuyu artists as some of their best Kikuyu worship songs are soul-touching.

Although the music entertainment industry is diverse, only those who offer their best can touch the hearts of their fans. Over the years, the Kikuyu gospel songs industry has experienced tremendous growth thanks to the numerous artists offering the best Kikuyu worship songs of different genres.

List of Kikuyu worship songs

If you are looking to worship the Lord with a Kikuyu song, check out the latest Kikuyu worship songs, as listed below.

1. Sammy Irungu - Ndukanjethe

Subscribe to watch new videos

If you are in search of the best worship songs in Kikuyu, Ndukanjethe by Sammy Irungu should be your go-to jam. The singer is telling people not to go looking for him where they left him yesterday because the Lord has blessed him and changed his location. The jam is a beautiful form of inspiration that God has the power to give us a new blessing every dawn.

2. Ruth Wamuyu - Ni Gukena

Ruth Wamuyu is known not only as an apostle but a great worshipper as well. Her latest track dubbed Ni Gukena is a message urging the people to praise and worship the Lord in their youth before they become old and without the energy to worship.

3. Betty Bayo - Wendo

Subscribe to watch new videos

Wendo by Betty is a fantastic Kikuyu Christian worship song talking about the love of Christ. Betty Bayo assures human beings that no worldly love can compare to the love of God. Unlike human love, God’s love is unconditional and available for all.

4. Shiru wa GP - Mugai

If you are looking for a worship song talking about the blessings of the Lord, Mugai by Shiru wa Gp is the track for you. Shiru wa GP is a renowned Kikuyu worshipper, and Mugai is one of her amazing pieces. The jam says that the blessings of the Lord know no boundaries, and He showers us with them every single day.

5. Sammy Irungu and Sammy K - Kiriro Giaku

Subscribe to watch new videos

While some artists offer great music as individuals, their collaboration with others offers nothing short of the best tunes. The collaboration between Sammy Irungu and Sammy K on the track Kiriro Giaku is nothing but amazing. Kiriro Giaku means your cry, and the song is an encouragement that the Lord has heard your cry, and He will be with you till the end.

6. Phyllis Mbuthia - Niatia Utangika

Subscribe to watch new videos

If you want to create a Kikuyu worship songs playlist, Phyllis Mbuthia’s Niatia Utangika is a must include. The translation of the title means, what can’t you do. The song is an assurance to all the believers that they are nothing that the Lord cannot do to them that believes in Him. For those feeling helpless, they should know that the Lord can do all that they trust Him for.

7. Grace Mwai - Hari Wee

Hari Wee, which means where you are, is a Kikuyu Christian worship track by Grace Mwai, which talks about the benefits of being in the Lord's presence. Grace Mwai assures the believers that there is hope in the Lord, and as long as they have confidence in him, they will experience his goodness in the land of the living.

8. Phyllis Mbuthia and Sammy Irungu - Muheani

Muheani, which means giver, is another excellent collaboration between Phyllis Mbuthia and Sammy Irungu. It is so popular that you will hear it in almost all Kikuyu worship songs mix. The artists talk about how the Lord does not forsake those who trust and wait upon Him because He is a beautiful giver.

9. Philip Kimani - Amukira Igongona Ria Ngoro Yakwa

Subscribe to watch new videos

Phillip Kimani’s worship song, Amukira Igongona Ria Ngoro Yakwa, is an excellent jam when you are seeking to recognize and offer thanks to God for His greatest blessing, giving up his son to die on the cross for the sinners. If you are in a thankful mood, this is the track to play as you thank the Lord for His amazing grace and love for the sinners.

Mucamo by Shiru wa GP is a fantastic worship song that talks about the feeling one receive when they become believers of Christ. Shiru tells us that the Lord is the lover who will never get tired of us, and He is also the giver of everything we need in life.
