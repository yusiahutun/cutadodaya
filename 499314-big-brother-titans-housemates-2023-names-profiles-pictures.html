Big Brother Titans housemates 2023: names, profiles, and pictures

Big Brother Titans is a reality TV show based on the Big Brother Franchise. In this show, contestants live in an isolated house and compete for a cash prize while being constantly monitored by cameras. The show initially had 20 housemates before being joined by four other contestants days later. The contestants compete in various challenges and are eliminated through a voting process by the public until the winner is found. So, how much do you know about Big Brother Titans housemates?

Big Brother Titans' theme is Ziyakhala Wahala. The show was first aired on 15 January 2023 on DSTV channel 198 and GOtv channel 29. It is scheduled to run for 72 days, and the winner will get a cash prize of $100,000. Big Brother Titans housemates names and countries of origin indicate that the show is a combination of Nigerian and South African young men and women.
