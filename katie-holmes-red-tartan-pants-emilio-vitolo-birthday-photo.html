Katie Holmes Wears Red Tartan Pants to Her 42nd Birthday Party

Fresh from becoming Instagram official, Katie Holmes stepped out with new boyfriend Emilio Vitolo to celebrate her 42nd birthday on Friday. As per usual, Holmes and Vitolo seemed to be extremely loved-up during the New York outing, and the Dawson's Creek icon looked seriously chic, too.

The actress wore a long black coat to keep warm in the snow, but she added a splash of color to the outfit with a pair of red tartan pants. She finished off the look with a chunky pair of black boots. Meanwhile, her culinary beau wore a blue blazer, with a black polo shirt and black pants. Both Holmes and Vitolo wore face masks throughout the outing.

Elder Ordonez / SplashNews.com

Vitolo decided to make his relationship with Holmes Instagram official this week in honor of the actress's birthday. Sharing a beautiful photo of them together, the chef wrote, "The most amazing, kindest, beautiful person ❤️. Every time I see your face it makes me smile . Happy Birthday !!! I love you !!" According to Hello! magazine, Holmes responded by commenting on the photo, and wrote, "Thank you so much my Love. I love u too!!!!!"

Holmes has been spotted with Vitolo on multiple occasions throughout the pandemic, as the New York-based chef owns a restaurant in the city. From being photographed dining with Vitolo's dad to shopping for groceries together to engaging in some heavy PDA, Holmes's new romance has most definitely been heating up during 2020. Basically, Holmes and Vitolo have become pretty much inseparable.
