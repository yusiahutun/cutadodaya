Remember Paris Hilton's Ex-Fiance Paris Latsis?! See Him 10 Years Later!

Talk about a blast from the past!

Paris Hilton's former flame Paris Latsis resurfaced at the Los Angeles Film Festival premiere of Lily Tomlin's latest click, "Grandma," this week. Can you believe it's been 10 years since the pair famously dated?!

Paris and Paris first hooked up in 2004, before he popped the question in May 2005. After a quick 5 month engagement, the then-24-year-old called things off and ... began dating Stavros Niarchos (another shipping heir) shortly after.

Remember, this was right around the time of "The Simple Life," at the heigh of Paris Hilton-mania.

Since 2005, both Parises have done pretty well for themselves though.

Latsis later dated Nikki Reed and has recently turned his eye towards film. Paris, now 32, served as producer on the new Lily Timlin flick, as well as "Machete Kills" and Cameron Diaz's "The Box."

While Hilton had a few legal issues and served time in jail from 2006-2007, she's gone on to become a global brand, with fashion and beauty lines and a music/DJing career.

These days, Ms. Hilton is currently dating another billionaire, Thomas Gross. The two were spotted locking lips on a yacht together in Spain this week.
