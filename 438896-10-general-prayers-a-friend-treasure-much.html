Prayer for healing for a friend

When one is unwell, you need someone to embrace them with love and concern. Showing a friend love through prayers is the best way to help them heal. The following are prayers you can use as a prayer for healing for a friend.

Dear Lord, you know how much I love and treasure my friend. At this time, she is unwell; I pray that you may heal her. You are a great physician, and nothing puzzles you. Please, Lord, specially visit her. You created every part of my friend, and may You please touch each aching part. May my friend experience Your favour through healing. Lord, I know that it shall be well with my friend, and soon we shall celebrate Your doings. I trust You fully for total healing. In Jesus name, I pray, Amen.By the stripes of Your son Jesus, we receive healing. Today in a unique way, I remember my ailing friend. Before she was born, you knew her. Father, may You stretch your healing hand upon her. May the blood of Jesus cover my friend always. Lord, you know the root cause of the ailment. May You do what no man can do. Dear Lord, may all the medication she is taking be of help. I declare that my friend will not die but shall live to tell of your goodness. Since I can do nothing about her sickness, may You visit her for me. Heal her for me, dear Lord. I pray in faith and the name of Jesus, Amen.
