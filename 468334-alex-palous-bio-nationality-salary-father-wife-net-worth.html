In 2017, he moved to Japan, where he competed in the Japanese Formula 1 for Threebond with Drago Corse. Some of the races in which he participated were:

  • 2019 – Super GT and Super Formula, where he competed full-time with McLaren Customer Racing Japan and TCS Nakajima Racing, respectively.

Indycar Series

Later, he tested an IndyCar in July 2019 with Dale Coyne Racing at Mid-Ohio. On 19th December 2019, it was announced that he would move to the IndyCar Series for a rookie campaign. In his first race for the team at the 2020 Genesys 300 at Texas Motor Speedway, he was taken out by fellow rookie Rinus Veekay.
