Early life

The footballer was born Rayne Dakota Prescott on 29 July 1993 (age 29 as of 2022) in Sulphur, Louisiana, United States. He is the son of Nathaniel Prescott (father) and Peggy Prescott (mother). Dak Prescott’s siblings are Jace Prescott, Ted Prescott, Natalie Prescott-Smith, and Elliot Prescott.

His parents divorced when he was young and had started school. As a result, his mother raised him as a single parent. Despite their separation, Dak admits that his father was always there for him.

Unfortunately, Dak’s mother and brother passed away. His mother died in 2013 after fighting cancer, while his brother, Jace, died at 31 after committing sui*cide in 2020. Dak attended Houghton High School. After high school, he joined Mississippi State University.

He graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Educational Psychology in December 2014. However, he did not stop studying. Instead, he pursued a Master’s Degree in Workforce Leadership a year later and earned it in December 2015 from the same institution.

What is Dak Prescott's ethnicity?

His ethnicity is multiracial. His father is African-American, and his mother is of European-American descent. Furthermore, Dak has Native- American roots. His maternal grandfather, Glyndell Wilbert, was a member of the Choctaw-Apache Tribe of Ebarb.

The Choctaw-Apache Tribe of Ebarb is also known as the Choctaw-Apache Community of Ebarb. They are a state-recognised tribe and non-profit organisation in Louisiana, United States. Although they are a tribe in Zwolle, Louisiana, they are not federally recognised as a Native-American tribe.
