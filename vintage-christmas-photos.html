Photos of Vintage Style Icons During Christmas

Clara Bow, 1920

Bettmann//Getty Images

Silent film star Clara Bow poses for a Christmas promotional photo in a festive velvet shawl.

Mary Pickford,1920

General Photographic Agency//Getty Images

Canadian actress Mary Pickford poses as a carol singer in a lacy frock for an image that graced the front of a Christmas card.

Dolores Del Río, 1925

Irving Chidnoff//Getty Images

Mexican film star Dolores Del Río fights off the winter chill in a long fur coat, topping the look off with a head cap and a handful of beautifully wrapped Christmas presents.

Mary Brian, 1929

John Springer Collection//Getty Images

Actors Buddy Rogers and Mary Brian share a kiss under the mistletoe. The actress is wearing a chiffon ball gown with a bateau neckline and festive satin trimmings.

Bette Davis, 1930

Keystone-France//Getty Images

Bette Davis wears a fur-trimmed cape and cap, just like Mrs. Claus herself, as she poses inside a giant wreath during the holidays.

Norma Talmadge, 1930

Keystone-France//Getty Images

Norma Talmadge and actor Gilbert Roland look cozy piled under fur blankets in a sleigh, while riding through Chamonix, France.

Ginger Rogers, 1932

Bettmann//Getty Images

Looking sophisticated in a slinky white satin evening gown, Ginger Rogers attends a Christmas party in Los Angeles. Notice how her dress's silver beading matches the tinsel-strewn tree.

Douglas Fairbanks, 1932

Bettmann//Getty Images

Fairbanks and his actor son, Douglas Fairbanks, Jr., spend their holiday week skiing at California's Lake Arrowhead—while dressed in wool coats and loose-fitting pants.

Anna May Wong, 1932

Bettmann//Getty Images

While attending a Hollywood Christmas party at the the Christy Hotel, Anna May Wong wears a traditional Chinese gown.

Norma Shearer, 1932

Bettmann//Getty Images

You can't go wrong with a gilded gown at a holiday party, and Norma Shearer knew it. Here, the actress is dressed in a gold lamé coat dress while attending Gary Cooper's Christmas party.

Claudette Colbert, 1932

Bettmann//Getty Images

Actress Claudette Colbert certainly knew how to work this winter white satin gown, while chatting with Douglas Fairbanks, Jr. at a Christmas Benefit Show at the Shrine Auditorium.

Lupe Vélez, 1932

Bettmann//Getty Images

Lupe Vélez spends the holidays in Palm Springs, California. The Mexican actress kept things simple with a white short-sleeved button-down, shorts, and white Oxfords.

Jean Harlow, 1934

Virgil Apger//Getty Images

Jean Harlow spent the week before Christmas on set of the musical melodrama Reckless. Even while taking a snack break, the actress brought the glitz and glam she's known for in a shimmery white gown.

Shirley Temple, 1935

John Springer Collection//Getty Images

Shirley Temple shows off her holiday dress. The child star wore a Peter Pan-collared dress with a festive print and ribbon sash.

Julie Haydon, 1937

Clarence Paul Richee//Getty Images

Stage and screen actress Julie Haydon dresses to the nines in a cape and a long-sleeved gown to deck the halls.

Vivien Leigh, 1940

New York Daily News Archive//Getty Images

Vivien Leigh and her husband, Laurence Olivier, arrive in New York on Christmas Eve. The actress looks chic in an all-black ensemble, complete with a statement-making bow and matching handbag.

Carmen Miranda, 1944

CBS Photo Archive//Getty Images

While recording a CBS Christmas radio show, Carmen Miranda wears her hair in a printed headscarf, as well as a white long-sleeved button-down and high-waisted gingham pants.

Dolores Del Río, 1944

Ed Clark//Getty Images

Dolores Del Río bundles up in a mid-length fur coat and scarf to join her friends for a festive evening of Christmas carols.

Lena Horne, 1947

Bettmann//Getty Images

Lena Horne bundles up while sailing on the SS America during the holidays. Glimpses of the singer's velvet skirt can be seen under her high-collared overcoat, as she sails to New York for Christmas with her family.

Hattie McDaniel, 1947

CBS Photo Archive//Getty Images

Hattie McDaniel looks flush with yuletide joy as she decorates a Christmas tree with her Beulah costars.
