Jennifer Lopez Pairs a Sporty Look with Oversized Versace Shades

In her latest outing, Jennifer Lopez went back to basics for a casually cool street style look.

The multi-hyphenate was photographed in Los Angeles yesterday wearing a sporty all-black outfit consisting of a zip-up cropped turtleneck and leggings by Thirty Years. She accessorized with accents of gold, wearing a pair of white Nike Air Jordans with gold logos and carrying a black Hermès Birkin bag with gold hardware. The cherry on top, however, was her choice of eyewear: an oversized pair of Versace shield sunglasses, with the lenses tinted to a dark orange and purple ombré.

This isn't the first time that J.Lo has styled the statement eyewear. Earlier this week, she rung in the start of summer by posting a series of backyard bikini photos, in which she posed in the Versace shades plus a black triangle bikini set from ViX, nude sandals from her JLO JENNIFER LOPEZ footwear line, and a floral-and-butterfly kimono from Kim+Ono.

"Summer mode: activated," she captioned the heated photos.

J.Lo is in the midst of releasing her highly anticipated Netflix documentary, Halftime, which will offer fans a behind-the-scenes look into the making of her and Shakira's joint Super Bowl Halftime Show in 2020. The film is set to be released on June 14.

"Halftime offers an intimate peek behind the curtain revealing the grit and determination that makes Jennifer Lopez the icon she is, from her performances onscreen and on stages around the world, to her Super Bowl halftime show, to the recent presidential inauguration," a press release read. "Halftime serves as the kickoff to the second half of Lopez's life, as she lays bare her evolution as a Latina, a mother, and an artist, taking agency in her career and using her voice for a greater purpose."
