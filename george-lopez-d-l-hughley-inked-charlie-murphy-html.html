George Lopez and D.L. Hughley get inked for Charlie Murphy

George Lopez and D.L. Hughley got matching hand tattoos of the late Charlie Murphy's nickname 'Darkness' from Rick James at Manhattan's Fun City Tattoo on Thursday.

The 57-year-old brother of Eddie Murphy - who died April 12 after battling leukemia - was best known for his 'True Hollywood Stories' about the late Superfreak singer and others stars on Chappelle's Show.
