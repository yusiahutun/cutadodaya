Bobby Flay's Dating History: See Who He's Romanced Over The Years!

Apparently, Bobby Flay is quite the catch! The 54-year-old celebrity chef has romanced quite a few gorgeous ladies over the years. Though his three past marriages sadly didn’t work out (more on that below!), Bobby has seemingly bounced back just fine each time and has reportedly dated some pretty famous Hollywood stars including January Jones, Chelsea Handler, and even Giada De Laurentiis. 

Bobby was most recently linked to actress Heléne Yorke, who is 20 years his junior. Though we’re not totally sure if the two are still dating today, Heléne, 33, recently gushed about her relationship with Bobby during an episode of the Food Network star’s popular show Beat Bobby Flay. “So I’m going to let you in on a little secret. Last season I went to a [New York] Knicks game and I met Heléne. Now we kind of date,” Bobby joked on the series to which she replied, “[We instantly hit it off], I was like who’s this sweet guy? Who knew?”
