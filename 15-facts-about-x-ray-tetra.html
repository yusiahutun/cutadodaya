15 Facts About X Ray Tetra

Source: Aquadiction.world

The X Ray Tetra, also known as the Pristella maxillaris, is a fascinating species of freshwater fish that captivates aquarium enthusiasts and researchers alike. These small, peaceful fish are native to the Amazon River basin in South America and have become popular additions to home aquariums around the world. The X Ray Tetra gets its name from the translucent area near its gills that resembles an X-ray image. This distinct characteristic is not only visually striking but also serves as a form of camouflage in its natural habitat.
