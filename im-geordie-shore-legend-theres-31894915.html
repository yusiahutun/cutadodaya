'I'm a Geordie Shore legend and there's two co-stars I'd love to see make epic return'

Geordie Shore star James Tindale has revealed which one of his former co-stars he'd like to see make a huge comeback to the MTV show.

The 33-year-old reality star recently filmed the 24th series of the wild series alongside with the likes of Charlotte Crosby, Holly Hagan, Chloe Ferry and Nathan Henry, which is currently airing weekly on MTV and Paramount+.

A jaw-dropping 21 Geordies and five children feature across the season but Geordie Shore OG James says he would like to see two of his former cast members return.

Speaking to Daily Star, James revealed: "I would love to have Beau [Brennan] back because he was so energetic. He's the only person I'd love to come back. I would also personally like to see Gary [Beadle] come back because I'd like to see how much he's changed and how much he's grown.

Gary was part of the Geordie Shore family from when the show first started back in 2011 until 2017 and Beau appeared on the show from 2019 until 2020. Other stars who haven't returned for the latest series include Bethan Kershaw and Vicky Pattison.

Opening up about what fans can expected from the latest show, James explained: "It's definitely different now kids are involved. There is drama but there's also so many good moments and really heartfelt touching moments that us to be a part of together and see was unreal.

"When we get together now we really appreciate it. We all have a lot of love for each other. Geordie Shore for us has played a monumental part of our lives and I think the fact it's still going now we're grateful for it. We don't often see each other so any chance we get for us all to get back together and go on holiday somewhere it's a no brainer."

James will introduce his girlfriend Leah to fans this series as she plan to make a life-changing decision. He said: "I had a lot going on going into this series. I've got a new girlfriend, we've been together like a year, so she came into the series and got to meet everyone.

"She had only met a couple of people outside of it so coming in and meeting everyone went really well. I think it shows how much we've all grown up. It couldn't have gone better so I was glad to bring her in and show me in a relationship again. We live in Dubai now so we were getting ready to go and move over there at the end of filming. We had big parties and told people for the first time because I managed to keep it a secret which was hard, so that's all on the show too."
