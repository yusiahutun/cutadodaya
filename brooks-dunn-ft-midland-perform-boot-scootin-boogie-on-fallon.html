Brooks &amp; Dunn Ft. Midland Perform Boot Scootin Boogie On Fallon

Brooks & Dunn, with Midland perform on the Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon. (Andrew Lipovsky/NBC)

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — Country music icons Brooks & Dunn teamed up with rising stars Midland on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon to perform their take on the country classic Boot Scootin’ Boogie.

While the band Asleep At The Wheel scored a No. 1 hit with the track in 1990, it was penned by Ronnie Dunn and Brooks & Dunn included the track in their 1993 album Hard Working Man as well as their most recent album Reboot.

For their performance on Fallon, Midland frontman Mark Wystrach kicked off the festivities but Ronnie Dunn soon stepped up to the mic with backing vocals from Midland’s Jess Carson and Cameron Duddy, and Kix Brooks on harmonica.

Brooks & Dunn are lined up for a slew fair and festival dates this summer, as well as a residency with Reba McEntire at the Colosseum at Caesars Palace from June 26-July 6.

Midland is lined up for festival plays this summer as well, including the Tortuga Festival in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida and are on the road with their current Electric Rodeo headlining tour.
