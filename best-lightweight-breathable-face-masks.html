13 lightweight face masks that won't leave you feeling flustered

If you're looking for something featherlight then we've got you covered

    In 2020, face masks became the unexpected must-have accessory. Fast forward to 2021 and they're now part of everyday life. The pandemic continues – and face masks are still around, but for some of us, wearing a regular face mask isn't going to be comfortable. Why not opt for a lightweight face mask that is just as effective, but doesn't leave you feeling hot and bothered?

    We’ve gathered together the best breathable and cooling face coverings – and you can buy them all online.
