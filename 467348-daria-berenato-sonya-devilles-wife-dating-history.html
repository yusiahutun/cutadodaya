Who is Daria Berenato?

Sonya Deville was born on September 24, 1993, in Shamong, New Jersey, United States. This means that she is 29 years old as of 2022. At birth, her parents gave her the name Daria Rae Berenato.

The name of her mother is Debbie Miliano. She grew up alongside her two sisters named, Brianna Berenato and Taylor Miliano.

She enrolled at Seneca High School in Tabernacle, New Jersey for her high school education. She debuted her mixed martial arts career at the age of 16 years. The biggest break in her career came in 2015 when she signed a contract with WWE. She is currently performing on the Smackdown brand.
