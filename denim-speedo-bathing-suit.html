Denim Speedo Bathing Suits Exist and We Don't Know How to Feel

Warm-weather fashion trends can be questionable. From flip-flop socks to dad bod t-shirts, we thought we'd seen it all. But, surprise! Denim speedos have arrived to ruin summer.

While Speedo is technically a brand of high-performance swimwear, the word is synonymous with one style of bathing suit in particular: a tight, revealing brief. This infamous male bikini is blinding in a way that could only be compared to staring directly into the sun. For decades, the Speedo has been one of the most attention-grabbing swimsuits a man could wear. That is, until someone created an off-brand version that looks exactly like a pair of way-too-short jeans. Introducing: the "Jeado."

Shinesty

This Speedo lookalike is the summer trend that no one asked for, but everyone is talking about. The viral swimsuit is not actually made of denim, though the details look so real that you could've had me fooled.

A website called Shinesty.com is selling the speedos for $39 and the description for the Jeado will leave you with your jaw on the floor. Enjoy:

They say jorts are the above-ground pool of the shorts world. We'd be inclined to agree. Our denim print swim brief, also known as the Jeado ... is just like that ... except it is the above-ground hot tub of the swim brief world...or something like that. It is like eating a bag of chips in church. Everyone looks over at you with disgust, but deep down they want some too. And if you're really risky you'll combine those last 2 sentences, eating a bag of chips in church while wearing this denim swimsuit. You'll probably get tossed out, but you'll look and taste good doing it.
*These should be paired with caterpillar-style mustaches for optimal results.

If you have one decent bone in your body, I beg of you: Do not, under any circumstances, eat a bag of chips in a church while wearing this denim swimsuit.

If you need me, I'll be hiding indoors until September.
