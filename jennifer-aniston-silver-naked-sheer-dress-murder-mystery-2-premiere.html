Jennifer Aniston Dazzles in a Silver Naked Dress at 'Murder Mystery 2' Premiere

Jennifer Aniston made quite an entrance at the Murder Mystery 2 premiere in Los Angeles last night.

The actress arrived at the Regency Village Theatre in a shimmering silver naked dress from Atelier Versace's spring/summer 2023 couture collection. The minidress was composed of woven silver, gray, and black string over a sheer nude fabric, embellished with tiny clear and black beads. She paired the piece with strappy black heels, a silver ring, and silver drop earrings.

Albert L. Ortega//Getty Images

Aniston's gray eye shadow perfectly matched her look, and she wore pink blush and a glossy pink lip. Her hair was styled down in relaxed waves.

Albert L. Ortega//Getty Images

While at the event, the actress posed for photos with longtime friend and Murder Mystery costar Adam Sandler. In true form, the actor arrived dressed in an oversized light gray New York Knicks hoodie from Todd Snyder layered over a bright pink-and-blue Hawaiian shirt, paired with beige pants and vibrant blue sneakers.

Michael Buckner//Getty Images

Sandler's—let's call it effortless—fashion launched a full street style movement last summer, with people everywhere embracing his go-to oversized hoodie, basketball shorts, and comfortable shoes uniform.

In an interview on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon last week, Aniston joked about Sandler's style and said that when he wears a suit, "he kind of looks like he's just playing dress-up" because "he just seems very uncomfortable."

Amy Sussman//Getty Images

The Friends alum also told Fallon about a particularly "nice dinner" she and Sandler were invited to in Italy, while doing press for their comedy film, to which Sandler showed up in basketball shorts.

"Satin basketball shorts with white piping, and Nike high-tops, and a turquoise velour [shirt] that had no relationship to the [bottom] half," she recalled, laughing.

Murder Mystery 2 will be available to stream on Netflix starting March 31.
