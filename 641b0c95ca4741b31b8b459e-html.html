Kay Hansen, a 23-year-old professional MMA fighter who recently left the UFC after three consecutive losses, has had to resort to posting content on OnlyFans, the uncensored adult website. She needs the money in order to train while recovering from injury. "It allows me to make the training schedule I want," Hansen told MMA Junkie. "I don't have to get another job working a 9-to-5, you know what I mean? So it allows me to structure my camps and focus on what really matters, and that's fighting." She is recovering from a broken hand so she can get back to fighting as soon as possible.
