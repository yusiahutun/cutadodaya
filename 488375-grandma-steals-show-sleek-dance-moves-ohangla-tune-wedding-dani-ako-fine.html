Grandma Steals Show with Sleek Dance Moves to Ohangla Tune During Wedding: &quot;Dani ako Fine&quot;

  • An elderly woman has won the admiration of internet users after a video of her dancing to a Ohangla song was posted online
  • The grandmother was at a wedding ceremony when he joined the dance floor with her walking stick and energetically danced to the tune
  • The woman was also showered with money as she faced before other women joined her on the dancing floor

An elderly woman took over the bridal dance at a wedding by outshining every other attendee at the ceremony despite walking with the help of a walking stick.

Dance to Ohangla

The grandma busted moves to a blaring Ohangla song, putting the newlywed couple to shame as they watched in disbelief from a distance

The video of the incident was shared on Facebook by comedian Mannerson Oduor Ochieng popularly known as Akuku Danger.
