Valentino Noir Rockstud Capsule Collection

When Valentino designers Maria Grazia Chiuri and Pierpaolo Piccioli rolled out their first Rockstud heels two years ago, they started an overnight sensation. Now collected (hoarded, even) by fashionistas everywhere, the Rockstud is as much a part of the brand's identity as Valentino red. This season, the duo highlights the new classic with the Noir capsule line of Rockstud shoes and bags in edgy black. Rock on.
