Introduction

JayzTwoCents (real name: Jason Langevin) is an American YouTuber and internet personality. He is best known for his YouTube channels “JayzTwoCents” and “JayzTwoCars.”

Jason vlogs about gaming and technology with a lighthearted slant.

The Origin Story

Langevin was born in Boston, Massachusetts, on June 3rd, 1981.

He grew up in Rialto, California.

He has stated that he had an interest in PCs from a young age, and began opening his PCs up at the age of ten to figure out what made them tick.

JayzTwoCents did not go into higher education; instead, he began to work in IT at the age of 16 as a computer networking consultant.

YouTube


Langevin’s first channel was entitled ‘JaysVFX,’ which he used to post Call of Duty montages and video edits. However, he quickly found this dissatisfying and believed that he had more to offer the world than simple VFX work.

He, therefore, abandoned his original channel to find his most famous one, ‘JayzTwoCents.’

On that YouTube channel, Langevin focused on technology – specifically how to build PCs and how to understand both the hardware and software aspects of PCs.

Langevin’s videos on the channel were noted for their casual and comedic aspects, which helped to make them accessible to the layman and ensure that casual viewers were not overwhelmed with esoteric technical jargon that might alienate them.

As the channel grew in popularity, Langevin branched out into other aspects of PC culture, including benchmarking new video-game titles, FPS counts on various games, exploring vintage technology such as gaming computers from the 1980s, and much more.

Langevin’s channel became a huge hit, and as of 2023 has 3.9 million subscribers.

Although he has since divided his attention between this and a new channel, Langevin still uploads several new videos a week, and enjoys a devoted fanbase.

In 2012, Langevin began his second channel, entitled “JayzTwoCars.” This channel was ostensibly devoted to cars, but there was a lot of crossover with Langevin’s personal life, and he would often post about his personal travails – including his struggles with weight loss.

Langevin also posted ‘slice-of-life’ videos to the channel. These included following him on mundane daily activities (such as grocery shopping) but also included family vacations, such as taking his kids to Disneyworld.

“JayzTwoCars” enjoyed moderate success, but did not reach the heights of his primary channel.

As of 2023, it has around 266,000 subscribers – a fraction of those enjoyed by JayzTwoCents.

Perhaps as a result of the relatively low number of viewers on this channel, then, it has been somewhat neglected since 2022, with no new uploads since June of that year.

Weight Problems

Langevin previously struggled with his weight a great deal, at one stage weighing over 500 pounds. 

In September 2019, he posted on his Twitter account:

”Haven’t posted anything fitness in a while. #goteamjay is still on course, and the fat loss and muscle growth is coming along nicely.” Jason added: ”Down 26lbs on top of whatever muscle I’ve gained. The recent drama has been great workout fuel! These are 180lb low rows.”

He eventually had weight-loss surgery in order to help him reach a healthy size.
