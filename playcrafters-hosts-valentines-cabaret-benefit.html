Playcrafters hosts Valentines cabaret benefit

As a special Valentine’s night fundraiser, Playcrafters Barn Theatre (4950 35th Ave., Moline) will present a variety of songs and stories of love and heartbreak in the new Love/Hurts Cabaret.

The event Wednesday, Feb. 14 at 7:30 p.m. is a benefit concert with a low $5 admission cost. Additional donations are graciously accepted.

Hosted by Eric Teeter, a longtime member of Playcrafters who has also been involved in other area productions and also the founder of One-off Theatrical Productions, the cabaret will entertain the audience with performances by many area talents — including Dolores Sierra, Jennifer Gregory, Skip Greer, Robert Gregory, Storm Marie Baca, Ben and Betsy Bergthold, Jonathan Turner, Sarah Potter, Jack Bevans, and Cindy Ramos.

Tickets are available at the door the night of the event or you can order online HERE. Concessions will be available.

Founded in 1929, Playcrafters is celebrating 95 years of quality theatrical entertainment with last weekend’s opening of “Harvey,” which continues next weekend. Call 309-762-0330 or go to playcrafters.com to order your tickets in advance.
