Kylie Jenner Wore the Easy Top That Pairs Well With Everything

Kylie Jenner has finally been showing off her beau, Timothée Chalamet, from dancing with him at Beyoncé’s Renaissance tour to PDA-filled moments at the U.S. Open. But what really grabbed my attention was Jenner’s easy-to-wear shirt during her latest outing.

At the tennis championships, Jenner sported an all-black tee featuring a round neckline. I love that she reached for a closet staple, because it’s simple, practical, and versatile. Wear a similar version, like this $8 Hanes find, with a pair of jeans for an effortless look, or dress it up with a maxi skirt and heels. Black tees like Jenner’s pair well with practically everything, taking the guesswork out of the fashion equation.

Her uncomplicated top is also multi-seasonal, working seamlessly with every single temperature and aesthetic. Layer on an olive green utility jacket for the toasty fall season, or wear it as is with a pair of shorts during the summer. You’ll reach for the timeless top again and again, only increasing its value. I found six spot-on lookalikes to get the look below, starting at just $8.

Multi-Seasonal, Easy Black T-Shirts 

Skims Stretch Cotton T-Shirt

I wouldn’t be surprised if the top Jenner was wearing was from her sister’s Skims clothing line. Whether she was donning the brand or not, this Skims Cotton T-Shirt looks pretty identical. The lightweight material is soft and cozy, while the fitted silhouette hugs your curves and can enhance your body’s shape. But my favorite part is the stretchy material, which never feels restrictive or too tight.  

Amazon Essentials Crewneck T-Shirt Pack of Two

What’s better than one staple top? Two, obviously. This two-pack of Amazon Essentials tees come out to $10 apiece, making it quite a steal. Look forward to a crew neckline, comfortable fabric, and a form-fitting look. One shopper even called the tops their “go-to tees,” while another said they still haven’t faded after “about 15 washes.”  

Madewell Brightside Rib T-Shirt

You can never go wrong with Madewell, and the brand’s Brightside Rib T-Shirt is simply further proof. This top sets itself apart thanks to its soft ribbed fabric, adding a bit of texture to the overall look. The shrunken, cropped design also makes it perfect for lounging in at home with a pair of sweatshorts.
