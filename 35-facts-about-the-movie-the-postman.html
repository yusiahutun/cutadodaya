35 Facts about the movie The Postman

Source: Amazon.com

The Postman is a critically acclaimed 1997 film that has gained a cult following over the years. Directed by Kevin Costner, the movie takes place in a post-apocalyptic future where society has collapsed, and communication between survivors is rare. The story follows a drifter who stumbles upon a long-abandoned postal truck and takes on the identity of a postman in order to bring hope and unity to the scattered communities. The film explores themes of love, courage, and the power of communication in a world devoid of hope.
