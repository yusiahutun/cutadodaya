Family Life 

As you already know, her dad is a renowned American actor Eddie Murphy. Her mother is Nicole Mitchell Murphy, who is an entrepreneur and a former model.

Bria's parents tied the knot on March 18, 1993. The celebrity duo was married for thirteen years before getting a divorce on April 17, 2006.

Besides the model, the two shares Miles Mitchell Murphy (born on November 07, 1992), Shayne Audra Murphy (born on October 10, 1994), Zola Ivy Murphy (born on December 24, 1999), Bella Zahra Murphy (born on January 29, 2002).

The "Amateur Night" actress also has five half-siblings from his father's past and present affairs. Her siblings are Eric Murphy (born on July 10, 1989), Christian Murphy (born on November 29, 1990), Angle Murphy Brown (born in 2007), Izzy Oona Murphy (born in 2016), and Max Charles Murphy (born in 2018).
