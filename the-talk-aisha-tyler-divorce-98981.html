The Talk Star Aisha Tyler and Husband Jeff Tietjens File for Divorce After 22 Years of Marriag

Aisha Tyler‘s 22-year marriage is officially over.

According to TMZ, the 45-year-old The Talk co-host’s husband, Jeff Tietjens, filed for divorce from the star earlier this month.

Aisha and Jeff married in 1994 and announced their separation in January 2015. The former couple do not have any children together.

Aisha (second from right) with her The Talk costars. (Photo Credit: Getty Images)

The TV star has previously been vocal about her inability to conceive a baby with Jeff on The Talk. “The hardest part, I think, is I really love my husband,” Aisha said on the daytime talk show in 2013. “He’s such a good person. He would be such a good father.” It is unclear at this time if the couple’s fertility struggles led to their split.
