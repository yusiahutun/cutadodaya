May 10, 2024 Jenniffer Sheldon

Race Result | 20 Apr 2024 | Sha Tin | Race 1 RACING FOR CHARITY HANDICAP | HK Racing

Racing Incident Report

Horse No 1 NOBLE WIN (H195) Z Purton Jumped only fairly.

After the race, Z Purton reported that his mount made a loud respiratory noise during the event and gave ground in the Home Straight.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race found that horse to be a "roarer" which had previously been reported.

Horse No 2 ROBOT FIGHTER (H309) K De Melo Raced wide and without cover for the majority of the event.

The performance of ROBOT FIGHTER, which finished tailed out, was considered unacceptable.

Before being allowed to race again, ROBOT FIGHTER will be required to perform to the satisfaction of the Stewards in a barrier trial and be subjected to an official veterinary examination.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 3 V LOVE YOU (J048) B Thompson After the race, B Thompson reported that from a wide barrier he was requested to take up a forward position.

He said racing in blinkers for the first time V LOVE YOU showed more pace than expected and when caught wide he elected to allow his mount to stride forward to lead the event.

Horse No 4 COOL BLUE (H011) K Teetan Near the 350 Metres was bumped by GREEN LASER which shifted out to improve.

After the race, K Teetan reported that his mount failed to run out today's race distance of 1650 Metres.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 5 FORTUNE PRESIDENT (G054) B Avdulla Slow to begin.

Horse No 6 M M NEBULA (G279) M Chadwick Near the 1500 Metres, when being shifted in to obtain cover, was awkwardly placed close to COOL BLUE and got its head up when being steadied.

Briefly held up making the Home Turn.

After the race, M Chadwick advised that from a wide barrier he was instructed to ride his mount in a conservative manner in the early stages.

Trainer P C Ng confirmed the instructions and stated that he issued such instructions to ensure that M M NEBULA was able to obtain cover.

Horse No 10 CHARMING STEED (E494) C Y Ho Sent for sampling post-race.

Horse No 11 CHIRON (E151) K C Leung Jumped only fairly and from a wide barrier was shifted across behind runners in the early stages.

After the race, K C Leung reported that his mount did not travel well at any stage of the event and raced in a disappointing manner.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 13 EAGLE RUN (G415) Y L Chung Sent for sampling post-race.

Horse No 14 GREEN LASER (E409) M L Yeung Shifted out near the 350 Metres and bumped with COOL BLUE.
