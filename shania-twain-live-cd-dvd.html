Shania Twain Releasing Live CD/DVD Combo

Country music vet Shania Twain has been rocking the Colosseum at Caesar’s Palace in Las Vegas for the past two years, pulling out all the stops for 105 performances of her 'SHANIA: Still the One' stage show. Now, she’s bringing the experience to a whole new audience.

A live DVD and CD of the show, produced by Twain herself, is hitting Walmart shelves only on March 3, so fans can bring the fun and flare of the Vegas show to the comfort of their own home.

“This show has been my labor of love for over three years,” Twain said in a news release. “It has meant so much to me and was such a privilege to live out my visions on that stage, and it is so exciting to now get to share the whole experience with the fans all over the world!”

In addition to the full show, 'SHANIA: Still the One Live from Vegas' features more than an hour of exclusive behind-the-scenes footage, providing a glimpse into the detail and hard work that goes into developing such a unique production, including costumes, rehearsals, training and more. The performance comes to life with a 13-piece band, trained horses, dancers, and even a flying motorcycle, in true Vegas style.
