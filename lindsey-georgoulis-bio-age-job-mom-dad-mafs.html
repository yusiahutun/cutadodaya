He admitted that meeting all the guys and having that closeness between them was comforting. “I knew going into it I’d find a wife, but no expectation to make friends,” he added.

Lindsey’s MAFS husband, Mark Maher, aka “Mark The Shark”,  was working as the CEO of Switch-Ups that he founded in Dec 2011. Also, he was the sales manager of CycleBar at the time.
