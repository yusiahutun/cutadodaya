MAFS: Jake Edwards shares his dental transformation in dramatic before and after photos

Jake Edwards is the latest reality star to undergo a $26,000 dental transformation, after admitting his teeth were his biggest insecurity.

On Tuesday, the 33-year-old former AFL star underwent a complete reconstruction of his smile - and the results are certainly impressive.

Before and after photos obtained exclusively by Daily Mail Australia show a dramatic change to Jake's pearly whites, thanks to veneers on his upper teeth.

Jake had seven Picasso Porcelain Veneers and four Picasso Porcelain Crowns valued at $26,000. 

Jake opted for a 'natural' style, and is understood to have wanted something that simply improved the look of his damaged teeth.
