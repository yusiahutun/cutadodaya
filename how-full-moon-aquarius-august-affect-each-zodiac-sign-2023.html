Full Moon in Aquarius Will Affect Every Zodiac Sign 2023

What inspires you when looking towards the future? With the sun blazing through its sign of rulership, Leo, there’s an aura of playfulness and passion in the air. Authenticity is our greatest asset, especially when considering how the full moon in Aquarius of August 2023 will affect each zodiac sign. That said, if you’ve been contemplating your colorful musings, or wondering how you can cultivate your talents for the greater good, you’ll wanna check where the astrological axis of Leo-Aquarius is located on your birth chart. After all, in addition to witnessing your manifestations come into fruition, full moons illuminate what’s been hidden from our conscious mind.

This year’s full moon in Aquarius will take place on August 1 at 2:31 p.m. ET, and via nine degrees of this fixed air sign. For reference on your full-circle moment, think back to the new moon in Aquarius that took place on January 21, 2023. How has your social atmosphere evolved in the past six months? Reflect on the dynamic of your community affairs, as well as your sense of belonging in the world. The last air sign in the zodiac, the essence of Aquarius speaks to everything from one’s inner genius to their unique eccentricities. Its Uranian archetype is symbolic of our ideals, higher mind, and visions of the future. So, amidst the sun’s journey through show-stopping Leo—bringing emphasis to your inner child and heart’s desires—the Aquarius full moon is putting a spotlight on your social environment, and clarifying whether it’s in support of your individual freedom. 

A symbol of enlightenment, celebration, and culmination, the sun makes a direct opposition to the moon during this lunar phase, which also serves as a call for balance between the divine feminine and sacred masculine energies within us. It’s also important to note the influence of Venus retrograde in Leo, especially now with the South Node transiting Venus-ruled Libra. Whether financially or in terms of our relationships, this is encouraging us to get clear on our heart’s desires and unique value systems. Do your current relationships empower you, or trigger your inhibitions? Whether personally or professionally, we can no longer disregard the areas of life that lack an equal amount of give and take.

When taking a closer look at this lunation’s astrology, it’s important to consider the influence of Aquarius’ co-rulers, Saturn and Uranus. Saturn, for instance, is currently retrograde in Pisces, and Uranus will soon follow the taskmaster’s footsteps, beginning its annual retrograde journey in Taurus on August 28. While transiting methodical Virgo, the messenger planet will make a direct opposition to Saturn retrograde, triggering feelings of uncertainty, caution and/or general insecurity. That being said, if you’re feeling stifled or inhibited when communicating, don’t apply more pressure onto yourself. There’s a time and place for everything, so take a step back if you’re feeling skeptical.

Moreover, the full moon will face off with Jupiter—planet of abundance, expansion, prosperity, and wisdom—whilst transiting the sensually driven sign of Taurus. This is not only magnifying the energy of this lunation, but also leading us towards brand-new avenues and opportunities for growth. Something is slowly but surely becoming more “real” and tangible, but there’s more. Mars will simultaneously harmonize with bountiful Jupiter—via an earthy and productive trine—which, in turn, energizes and supports our decisions moving forward. Venus retrograde’s square to Jupiter, however, adds a layer of shock and unconventionality to the mix, which is very Aquarian, so expect the unexpected!
