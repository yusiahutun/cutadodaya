Catch the Wave of Hope Gala

Denise Serls, Mona Salisbury, Rosa Castillio and Lluvia Castillo

Catch the Wave of Hope hosted its second annual gala at the Yacht & Country Club of Stuart. Survivor Rosa Castillo shared her story of being trafficked with more than 100 guests. The organization is raising awareness about human sex trafficking in South Florida. Its goal is to open a home locally for victims of human sex trafficking.
