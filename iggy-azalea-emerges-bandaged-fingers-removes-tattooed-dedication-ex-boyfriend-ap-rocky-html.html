Iggy Azalea emerges with bandaged fingers after removing A$AP Rocky tattoo dedication

Her fiancé may well be keen to slip a ring on her finger, but Iggy Azalea appeared keen to wipe something off it during an appearance in Los Angeles on Wednesday. 

The Australian rapper emerged with thick bandages swathed around three fingers on her left hand, suggesting she is keen to remove her tattooed dedication to ex-boyfriend ASAP Rocky.

Iggy previously wore the words Live and Love on two of her fingers, followed by the acronym A$VP on a third - before famously tattooing a cross over the latter following her split from Rocky in 2012.
