Tiger | COD Warzone and Vanguard Weapon Blueprint

The Tiger is a Weapon Blueprint available in Call of Duty: Vanguard and Call of Duty: Warzone Pacific.

It is a Legendary blueprint variant of the base weapon STG44, one of the Assault Rifles featured in Call of Duty.

The Tiger blueprint was released in Season 1 (VG) as part of the Bundle Year of the Tiger.
