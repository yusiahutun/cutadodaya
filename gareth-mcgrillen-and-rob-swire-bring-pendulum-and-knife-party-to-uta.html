Gareth McGrillen And Rob Swire Bring Pendulum And Knife Party To UTA

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — United Talent Agency announced the signing of electronic musicians Pendulum and Knife Party for North American and Canadian representation.

Pendulum, the drum & bass project founded by Rob Swire along with original members Gareth McGrillen and Paul Harding, is lined up to close out the main stage at the UK electronic music festival South West Four at the end of August

The show will mark the first time all three original members of the group have performed outside of Australia in more than a decade.

At the same time, Knife Party, which Swire co-founded with Gareth McGrillen, are supporting the release of the latest EP, Lost Souls, which is the duo’s first new material in almost four years.

As well, wire and McGrillen recently launched their own podcast, The Knifecast. Special guests featured on the prodcast have included Jauz, Sub Focus, Kill The Noise, Muzzy, and Rage Against the Machine’s Tom Morello, among others.

Steve Gordon and Kevin Gimble, UTA’s Co-Heads of Electronic Music, will be leading the team for both Knife Party and Pendulum and they are managed by Decade Management.
