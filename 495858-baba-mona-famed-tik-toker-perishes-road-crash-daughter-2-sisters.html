Baba Mona: Famed TikToker Perishes in Road Crash With Little Daughter, 2 Sisters

  • Kevin Oselu, who was known for his content twined with humour, died with his daughter and two sisters on Friday, February 24
  • Baba Mona, as he was fondly referred to, was driving a saloon car which veered off at a spot in Londiani where it rolled severally
  • The goner will be remembered for the pieces of advice he gave to all and sundry through his TikTok account, which has an enormous audience

Kenyans have been gutted about the death of Kevin Oselu.

The famed content creator, who had a massive fanbase on TikTok perished in a road crash on the evening of Friday, February 24.

Tragic end for Baba Mona and kin

Commonly and fondly known as Baba Mona, the father of one was driving from Nairobi to Kisumu while in the company of his young daughter and two sisters.

According to sources, he was about to branch to Awasi to drop off the two siblings when the black saloon car they were aboard veered off the road and rolled severally.

This is after he lost control of the car, which ended in a culvert near the Kipkelion coffee mill in Koru.

The quartet perished on the spot, as confirmed by one Leonard Ochieng', Baba Mona's close friend.

"Their vehicle got involved in a crash when they were near Londiani. He was headed to Awasi to drop off his two sisters before coming to returning Kisumu. He had charged me with booking for him an Airbnb which he was to stay in while in Kisumu. I know only one of his sisters named Connie," said Leonard who spoke with this publication.

Tragic surgery for Ugandan TikToker

Still in obit matters, another content creator died recently following a medical operation.

Asia Namirembe, of Ugandan descent, who was popularly known as Asia wa Charles, died in late January after surgery.

Her territorial nature with her partner Charles shot her to fame a couple of years ago.

Her audience loved her for the open-mindedness and affection she publicly treated her fiance with.

The couple used to create content together; hence her nickname Asia wa Charles.

She had been complaining of severe stomach pains for about a week; she was later rushed to hospital, where the doctors advised immediate surgery that would turn tragic.
