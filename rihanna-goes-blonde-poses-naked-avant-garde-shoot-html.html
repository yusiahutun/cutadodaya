Rihanna goes blonde and poses NAKED for avant garde shoot

Rihanna has posed naked for an avant garde new photo shoot. 

The 33-year-old Bajan artist stripped off for a new spread in Dazed magazine, which features three different covers shot by Rafael Pavarotti.  

In a collaborative photoshoot styled by Ib Kamara the star pulled off ten jaw-dropping looks for the pages of the subversive publication.  

Naked ambition: Rihanna stripped down for Dazed magazine, wearing nothing but a flowing platinum blonde wig styled in thick threaded locks to cover her modesty 

Showstopper: The star's new spread in the magazine features three different covers

On one cover the megastar strikes a strong pose, accentuating her every curve in a custom Burberry metallic gold catsuit and heels.

Her black hair is in a shaggy mullet as she stares seductively into the camera.

The headline reads: 'The Golden Reign of Rihanna.' 

Alternate look: Another variation reads, 'The Reign Never Stops,' as the singer-turned-mogul leans on a cane in an all-white look

Another variation reads, 'The Reign Never Stops,' as the singer-turned-mogul leans on a cane in an all-white look while two men (Omar Sesay and Alton Mason) stand behind her dressed in black, holding an umbrella over her head. 

Riri models a custom Burberry string bikini and thigh high boots underneath a floor-length trench coat.

For each concept the billionaire's makeup looks were created by Daniel Salstrom using none other than Fenty Beauty and Fenty Skin products. 

The hairstyles for the shoot were styled by Yusef Williams.

High level: A third cover reads, 'New Heights,' as the songstress is seen sporting a green Louis Vuitton baseball cap atop an extremely high crop of hair 

Finally, a third cover reads, 'New Heights,' as the songstress is seen sporting nothing but a green Louis Vuitton cotton mesh baseball cap atop an extremely high crop of hair, à la Marge Simpson.

Inside the celebratory issue the show-stopping fashion moments continue with the pop star wearing a billowing blood red gown and matching cylindrical headpiece.

Another image sees the 'Work' singer draped in denim as she dons an off-the-shoulder, floor-grazing coat lined with white feather trimming at the collar and frayed at the cuffs and along the bottom hem.

The statement piece is combined with a blue jean skirt and black heels that boast white knee-high laces. For the look, Kamara - who's also Editor-in-Chief- of the mag - added a white headscarf, topped with a tall, flat-brimmed white hat.

Ms. Fenty uses another cane, this one cream-colored, as a prop. 

Red vision: The show-stopping fashion moments continue with a billowing blood red gown and matching cylindrical headpiece

Denim moment: Another image sees the 'Work' singer draped in denim as she dons a floor-grazing coat lined with white feather trimming

Moving on, the lingerie designer looks like a futuristic empress as she rocks a golden bodice and another elaborate accessory on her dome.

The metal headgear frames her face and features an exaggerated black mohawk with gold hair clips situated throughout. 

The ensemble continues with sheer, black, opera gloves topped with gold bangles that are nearly stacked to the full length of Rihanna's forearms. 

A long, high-waisted black skirt features a full-length split that exposes white polka dot tights and a pair of gold-toe heels. 

Bold: The lingerie designer looks like a futuristic empress as she rocks a golden bodice and another elaborate accessory on her dome

The next unprecedented look is 1920s flapper-meets-punk rock as the Savage x Fenty founder poses in a body-clinging little black dress with fringe cutouts at the bosom and thigh. 

The 5ft8in performer stands on knee-high black, studded platform shoes that she wears over black fishnet stockings. 

Another pair of black opera gloves adds to the look, as well as a black chandelier head-topper, and the fashion rule breaker's famous lips are coated in a velvety, vampy, matte red.

Balancing act: The 5ft8in performer stands on knee-high black, studded platform shoes that she wears over black fishnet stockings

The final look sees the unapologetic beauty stripped down, wearing nothing but a flowing platinum blonde wig that's styled in thick threaded locs that strategically cover her bits. 

It's paired with a strong makeup look featuring pencil-thin eyebrows and a bold red lip stain. 

The 30th anniversary issue of Dazed will be available for purchase Thursday, September 16.
