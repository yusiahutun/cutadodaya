Masker Singer Octopus is opera singer Lesley Garrett according to these clues

Fans of The Masked Singer only have to wait a few more hours to find out who is hiding under the famous masks of Queen Bee, Octopus and Hedgehog.

Octopus has had many names thrown her way, including Kylie Minogue, Sheree Murphy, Courtney Act, Leona Lewis, Aussie babe Holly Valance and Laura Whitmore.

Singer Lesley Garrett is the singer who has most clues pointing towards her following weeks of speculation

However, new clues suggest that the star speaks several languages, has had a stint in the Dales, is a sweetheart which could link to her early days of helping around RAF Lindholme as a 17-year-old.
