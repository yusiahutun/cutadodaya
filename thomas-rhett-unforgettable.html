Listen to Thomas Rhett, 'Unforgettable'

Thomas Rhett's new single "Unforgettable" is another remarkably contagious love song from a singer skilled in the art of the understatement.

There's a distinct, disarming charm that comes with the best Thomas Rhett songs, and while he flips between and across genres, he'll usually pull it back between the lines the next time. An acoustic riff pins this mostly organic new offering from his upcoming Life Changes album. "Unforgettable" is a breezy country train that picks up steam at each chorus before releasing a burst to begin a new verse. It's refreshing, and at barely two-and-a-half minutes, it leaves you wanting more.

With two songs shared from Rhett's third studio album, it's clear he's simply moving forward with the open-handed, inviting style that made him a country headliner. "Craving You" and "Unforgettable" are more advanced and maybe more mature songs that don't leave behind young audiences.

Did You Know?: Rhett admits this song doesn't describe his relationship with wife Lauren, as they've known each other for too long. But the song does get Lauren's approval. It's worth noting that their wedding anniversary is Oct. 12, not far from the date mentioned in the opening verse of "Unforgettable."
