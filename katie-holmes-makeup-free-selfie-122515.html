Katie Holmes is beautiful with and without makeup!

The 38-year-old actress took to Instagram on Wednesday, Jan. 4 to share a gorgeous makeup-free selfie — and we're seriously loving the new pic!

"Happy January ?," Katie captioned the snapshot.

In a previous interview with Hello! magazine, the mom-of-one — she shares 10-year-old daughter Suri Cruise with ex-husband Tom Cruise — dished on her beauty secrets.

"There are a few staples in my make-up bag. Mascara, lip gloss and concealer… When it comes to shades, I traditionally stick with nudes and light pinks but if the occasion calls for it, I like playing with a bold red lip or a dramatic eyeliner," the former Dawson's Creek star said.

"Generally I stick with nudes, browns and purples on my eyes. For every day, I like to stick with what I know works for my skin. I make bolder choices on the red carpets — things like smoky eyes or red lips," Katie continued.

"I regularly exfoliate and hydrate my skin as often as I can," she said. "I also drink a lot of water, and exercise to help keep my skin in order."

Katie's newest makeup-free selfie isn't the first time she's dared to go bare on social media. In mid-October, she similarly shared a fresh-faced photo of herself simply captioned with a kissy face emoji.

"Beautiful… such a beauty!" one fan commented on Katie's pic with another writing, "You never age, Katie — looking amazing!"
