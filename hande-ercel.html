Hande Erçel is a Popular Turkish actress and model who doesn’t need any kind of introduction. At the moment she is one of the most famous Turkish actresses in the world who has worked in so many television productions and several movies as well. However, she is popularly known for her role in Ask Laftan Anlanmaz, Sen Cal Kapimi, Gunesin Kizlari, and many other series.

Early Life

Hande Erçel was born on 24th November 1993 in Bandirma, Turkey. The names of her parents are Aylin Ercel and Kaya Ercel. She also has siblings named Gamze Ercel. At a young age, she showed interest in acting and modeling career. Hande has completed her high school education at a local school in Turkey. Further for her higher studies, she went to Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University. By looking at her career she must have taken classes to improve her acting and modeling skills.

In 2016 she was seen in Ask Laftan Anlamaz as Hayat Uzun and the very next year, she appeared in Siyah Inci as Hazal Sulabi/Naz Demiroglu. In 2019 she was seen working in series named Halka as Mujde Akay and Azize as Azize Gunay/Melek Aydin.

Along with the series she has worked with various advertisements and magazines. In 2020 she was seen in both series and films. The series she worked in is Sen Cal Kapimi as Eda Yildiz.
