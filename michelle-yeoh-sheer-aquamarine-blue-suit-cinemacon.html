Michelle Yeoh's Sheer Aquamarine Suit Is Perfect for Spring

The fresh breeze felt at CinemaCon in Las Vegas on Wednesday came from the stride of Michelle Yeoh dressed in a perfect bright blue suit.

The actor joined her costars in the upcoming film adaptation of Wicked at the event, where they chatted about the movie and took plenty of loved-up selfies. The actors embodied their characters with their looks, and Yeoh surely channeled Madame Morrible—headmistress of Crage Hall at Shiz University (the school where witches Elphaba and Glinda first meet as teens)—and her signature green-blue wardrobe.

Yeoh opted for an airy suit in a cool aquamarine—a set composed of a long double-breasted blazer in a gauzy sheer fabric, and dreamy loose trousers that swished as she walked. The actor wore the see-through blazer over a black bra. She finished the look with glossy sky-high beige platform heels and some diamond jewelry from Anita Ko, including a rope diamond ear cuff and several rings.

The Crazy Rich Asians star wore her hair in a side-parted, wavy style, and had on natural rosy makeup.

David Becker//Getty Images

Yeoh was joined by costars Ariana Grande, Cynthia Erivo, Jeff Goldblum, Jonathan Bailey, and Ethan Slater at the event. Grande channeled her character, Glinda, in a pink 3-D flower dress by Oscar de la Renta. Erivo, meanwhile, went as Elphaba (the future Wicked Witch of the West) in a green top, silver miniskirt, and thigh-high green boots—all from Versace’s Fall/Winter 2024 ready-to-wear collection.
