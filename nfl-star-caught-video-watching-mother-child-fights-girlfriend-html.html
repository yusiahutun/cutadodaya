NFL's Dante Fowler caught on video watching two women fight

Jacksonville Jaguars player Dante Fowler has been caught standing idly by as his girlfriend brawls with the mother of his child brawls, according to news reports.

Surveillance footage at a Florida apartment complex showed the 21-year-old acting as a referee between the pair as they argued outside an elevator on February 1.

But the defensive end then took a step back and appeared to gesture to the woman, in a grey T-shirt, by his side, who has been identified as his girlfriend byTMZ.
