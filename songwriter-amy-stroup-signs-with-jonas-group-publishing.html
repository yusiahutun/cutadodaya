Songwriter Amy Stroup Signs With Jonas Group Publishing

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess) – Jonas Group Publishing, a division of Jonas Group Entertainment, has added the talented and prolific artist Amy Stroup to its roster. Describing herself as a “song farmer,” Stroup has seen her songs recorded by various artists, including Chaka Khan and Ingrid Michaelson.

In a significant move, Jonas Catalog Holdings I, LLC, has acquired Stroup’s Danger Twins Catalog. This catalog boasts impressive stats, over 2.4 billion video views on TikTok and YouTube, and over 50 million global streams.

“I am thrilled to announce our collaboration with Amy Stroup and the Danger Twins catalog,” said Jonas Group Entertainment’s Founder, Kevin Jonas Sr. “Amy is a truly gifted artist whose creativity and musical genius align perfectly with our vision at Jonas Group Publishing and Jonas Catalog Holdings. I want to thank Access Media Advisory and Teresa Miles Walsh for their expertise and guidance on the acquisition of this catalog and their participation in financing, and I would also like to thank Moghan Music for providing valuable assistance throughout the purchase of the catalog.”

Stroup is renowned for her prowess in sync creation, with her music featured in various mediums such as TV shows like Grey’s Anatomy and This Is Us, films including Disney’s Willow, and ad campaigns for brands like British Airways, Google, and Nike.

“Amy is a fearless and innovative songwriter with an effortless coolness that transcends all musical boundaries,” said Jonas Group Publishing President Leslie DiPiero. “I believe she will inspire anyone she collaborates with. We are so grateful to partner and amplify her existing and future works.”

“I’ve been enamored with the art of songwriting since I was a kid in Texas,” said Stroup. “If I were to name a song or artist I love, Leslie DiPiero, Kevin Jonas, or Phil Guerini would likely have had something to do with it, and I am immensely excited to jump on board with the team at Jonas Group Entertainment. l can’t believe I get to keep living this dream and launch a whole new chapter.”

Apart from her career as an artist, Stroup recently released the critically acclaimed album “Since Frank” and collaborated with the duo Danger Twins. As an entrepreneur, she co-founded Milkglass Creative, a boutique studio providing branding and design services for a prestigious clientele, including recording artists and bestselling authors.
