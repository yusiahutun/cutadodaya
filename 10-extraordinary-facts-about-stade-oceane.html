The Roof of Stade Oceane Opens and Closes in Just 10 Minutes

One of the most impressive features of Stade Oceane is its retractable roof. Whether it’s sunny, rainy, or even snowing outside, the roof ensures that events can take place inside the stadium comfortably.

The Capacity of Stade Oceane is 25,178
