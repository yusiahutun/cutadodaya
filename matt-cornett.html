Matt Cornett Biography, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth

Matt Cornett is a teenage superstar from the USA who played the role of E. J. Caswell on the Disney+ series, High School Musical: The Musical The Series. He enjoys playing football, singing, playing guitar, video games, and spending quality with his family. Matt is still a rising star who mostly played recurring or supporting roles but still built a good reputation for himself.

Early Life

Matt Cornett was born in Rodgers, Arkansas on October 6, 1998. Talking about his family then his parents are Art and Rhonda Cornett while he has an older brother and a sister with whom he grew up in an idyllic atmosphere, and returns to that place, often, even after moving to Los Angeles in 2012. Though Matt has appeared in many interviews, he chooses to keep his school name and education a secret and has never revealed it or talked about it.

Matt Cornett Career

Matt started his career after he moved to South California. He was first seen on the screen after being featured in National Television commercials and print campaigns. He gained fame in the teen world after he appeared in a recurring guest star role as Zach Barnes on Nickelodeon’s “Bella and the Bulldogs.” His role as Zach, help him earn the Young Artist Award for Best Performance in a TV Series – “Recurring Young Actor” in 2016. Matt’s most recent role is when he played the lead role of E.J. in Disney+ High School Musical: The Musical: The Series, which was launched in 2019 and was streamed on the new Disney+.
