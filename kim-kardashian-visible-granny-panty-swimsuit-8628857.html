Kim Kardashian Wears Designer Granny Panties to the Beach

Kim Kardashian is no stranger to experimenting with her style, and her beach and vacation attire is no exception.

The Kardashians star is currently on vacation in Turks and Caicos with the entire family, kids included, partially in celebration of her niece True Thompson's sixth birthday. Kardashian's first photo from their remote location featured her wearing a white two-piece set that consisted of a sportswear-inspired bikini top and baggy, granny panty Miu Miu bottoms. To add some coverage to the ensemble, the SKIMS founder threw on a stretchy taupe skirt, also by the luxury Italian fashion brand.

Another set of swimsuit photos followed on April 7 — Kardashian wore a triangle bikini set that featured brown snake print. The 43-year-old accessorized with a large cowboy hat in black, officially signaling that she, too, is in her cowgirl era like the rest of us. "This ain’t Texas…it’s Turks," she captioned the set of images, referring to Beyoncé's latest country hit single, "Texas Hold 'Em."

By April 8, fans were treated to an entire carousel of snaps from the family. The set of pictures in the set featured Kim's daughter North West giving True a piggyback ride. Psalm, Chicago, and Saint all made cameos in the photos as well, taking walks on the white sandy beach, relaxing in a speed boat, and hanging out with some of their close friends while on the clear blue water. The last picture in the slide featured a hot pink blow-up balloon display that spelled out "Happy Birthday True." (True turns six years old on April 12.) "Spring break," she captioned the sweet images.
