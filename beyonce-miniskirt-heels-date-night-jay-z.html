Beyonc Pairs a Miniskirt with Towering Heels on Date Night with Jay-Z

Beyoncé and Jay-Z stepped out for date night this past weekend at celebrity hot spot Giorgio Baldi, the Santa Monica Italian restaurant favored by stars like Rihanna, Angelina Jolie, and—of course—the Carters.

Paparazzi photographed the couple as they made a rare show of PDA by holding hands. The "Break My Soul" singer embodied tenniscore with her outfit, wearing a navy blue crewneck from the Whitney Museum and a matching pleated miniskirt. She completed the look with towering tie-up stilettos from Femme LA and a monogram bag from Louis Vuitton.

Meanwhile, the rapper looked suave in an all-black outfit, composed of a T-shirt, a duster coat, trousers, and a beanie. He finished the ensemble with white Air Force 1s.

MEGA

With the Recording Academy announcing Grammy nominations earlier this month, Beyoncé made history as one of the most-nominated artists of all time—tied only by her husband. Both artists have 88 nominations under their belts.

Bey scooped up nine nominations for the 65th Annual Grammy Awards, including nods for her seventh studio album, Renaissance, in the categories of Album of the Year, Record of the Year, and Song of the Year.

The mogul previously hinted at the album's theme in an interview for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2021 cover story.

"With all the isolation and injustice over the past year, I think we are all ready to escape, travel, love, and laugh again. I feel a renaissance emerging, and I want to be part of nurturing that escape in any way possible," she said at the time. "I've been in the studio for a year and a half. Sometimes it takes a year for me to personally search through thousands of sounds to find just the right kick or snare. One chorus can have up to 200 stacked harmonies. Still, there's nothing like the amount of love, passion, and healing that I feel in the recording studio. After 31 years, it feels just as exciting as it did when I was nine years old. Yes, the music is coming!"
