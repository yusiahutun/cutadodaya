Ernie Lively Biography, Age, Height, Wife, Net Worth, Wiki

Ernie Lively was an American actor and acting coach. He was known for his legendary 5-decade film and TV career. In his long career, he had amassed more than 100 credits. He was mostly known for his roles in Mulholland Falls, The Beverly Hillbillies, Passenger 57, The Man in the Moon, and Shocker. He also played the role of father of his real-life daughter, Blake Lively in The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants franchise.

Early Life

Ernie Lively was born on January 29, 1947. His birth name was Ernest Wilson Brown Jr. which he later changed to Ernie Lively. He was the son of Beatrice Gray and Ernest Brown Sr. Lively was born in Baltimore, Maryland. It is upsetting that he is no more with us anymore. He left us on the 3rd of June 2021.

Ernie Lively Career

Lively began his career with guest roles on popular series such as The Waltons and Fantasy Island. Later on, he appeared in many tv series like McCloud, Private Benjamin, CBS Schoolbreak Special, The Dukes of Hazzard, Fame, Misfits of Science, Remington Steele, Alfred Hitchcock Presents, Warm Hearts Cold Feet, Hill Street Blues, and many more. His first appearance in a tv movie was in The Million Dollar Dixie Deliverance where he played the role of Danny. In 1980 he acted in Drop-Out Father. In 1986, he appeared in The Children of Timesquare. After 1987, he flourished, bagging many roles with his spectacular performance. From then on he acted in the TV series Newhart and Midnight Caller. The year 1990 was a hit for him as he appeared in many movies.
