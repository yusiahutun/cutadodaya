Jennifer Aniston shares rare childhood photo for poignant reason

The Morning Show star wrote an emotional message to accompany the photo

    Jennifer Aniston celebrated her 54th birthday on Saturday, marking the day with an emotional post about her parents on Instagram.

    The Morning Show star shared a sepia-hued photo of herself as a child, alongside her parents John Anthony Aniston and Nancy Dow, writing: "Thinking about these two humans that created me as I bask in the afterglow of a beautiful birthday.
