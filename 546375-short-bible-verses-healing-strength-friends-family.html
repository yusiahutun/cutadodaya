Short Bible verses for healing and strength for friends and family

In the journey of faith, amidst life's trials and tribulations, finding solace and strength in the words of Scripture can be a profound source of comfort. These concise yet potent words can uplift and empower, offering solace in times of need and fortitude in moments of weakness. Discover the transformative power of short Bible verses for healing and strength, which provide guidance, hope, and resilience in times of adversity.

Bible verses about healing sickness

Bible verses to comfort the sick offer hope and peace during illness. Here is a list of scriptures you can read or send your loved ones when afflicted with sickness and diseases.

  • Matthew 8:14-15: Jesus went home with Peter and saw Peter's mother-in-law lying in bed with a fever. He touched her hand, and the fever left her. Then she got up and served them.
  • Matthew 9:20-22: A woman bleeding for twelve years came up behind Jesus and touched the hem of his clothes. She thought, 'If I only touch his robe, I'll be healed.' When Jesus turned and saw her, he said, 'Be encouraged, daughter. Your faith has healed you.' And the woman was healed from that time on.
  • Isaiah 53:5: But he was pierced for our transgressions and crushed for our iniquities; the punishment that brought us peace was on him, and by his wounds we are healed.
  • James 5:14-15: If any of you are sick, they should call for the church's elders, and the elders should pray over them, anointing them with oil in the name of the Lord. Prayer that comes from faith will heal the sick, for the Lord will restore them to health. And if they have sinned, they will be forgiven.
  • Malachi 4:2: But the sun of righteousness will rise on those revering my name; healing will be in its wings so that you will go forth and jump about like calves in the stall.
  • Isaiah 38:16-17: The Lord Most High is the one who gives life to every heart, who gives life to the spirit! Look, he indeed exchanged my bitterness for wholeness. You have spared my whole being from the pit of destruction because you have cast all my sins behind your back.
  • 2 Chronicles 7:14: If my people who belong to me will humbly pray, seek my face, and turn from their wicked ways, I will hear from heaven, forgive their sin, and heal their land.
  • Exodus 15:26: He said, if you listen carefully to the Lord your God and do what is right in his eyes, if you pay attention to his commands and keep all his decrees, I will not bring on you any of the diseases I brought on the Egyptians, for I am the Lord, who heals you.
  • Acts 28:8-9: Publius' father was bedridden, sick with a fever and dysentery. Paul went to see him and prayed. He placed his hand on him and healed him. Once this happened, the rest of the sick on the island came to him and were healed.
  • Matthew 8:1-3: Now, when Jesus came down from the mountain, large crowds followed him. A man with a skin disease came, kneeled before him, and said, 'Lord, if you want, you can make me clean.' Jesus reached out his hand and touched him, saying, 'I do want to. Become clean.' Instantly, his skin disease was cleansed.
