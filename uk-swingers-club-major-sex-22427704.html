UK swingers club major sex festival faces being shut down for breaking social distancing

A UK swingers club is planning a major sex event for next month, but it could be shut down for breaking social distancing measures.

HU9, Hull's only swingers club, is holding its annual Swing Fest in August.

It will feature a large stage, a light show, DJs, tribute acts, an "adult bouncy castle" and even fire breathers.

But now one swinger, who is also an NHS worker, has claimed the event is unsafe as none of the attendees will be social distancing.

The private members club says its event is for swingers only and has been advertised on swingers websites.
