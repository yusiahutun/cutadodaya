Audiences Returning To Some City Winery Locations In October

Carbon Leaf performs at City Winery Atlanta (James Brim)

(CelebrityAccess) –Live music will return to a number of City Winery locations around the U.S., starting in October.

The shows will feature limited audiences of just 50 people with seating plans designed to provide social distancing.

The return of live concerts starts on October 3rd in Chicago with a performance by Goran Ivanovic & Fareed Haque. Other announced live audience shows include a pair of performances by Mavericks frontman Raul Malo in Atlanta on October 10th.four shows from Ken Ford on October 17 & 17 and a performance by Ralph Stanley II & The Clinch Mountain Boys on Nov. 1.

The performances will be conducted in accordance with local health rules and patrons will be required to wear a mask upon arrival, when finished eating, or drinking, and when moving around the room.

As well, the shows will be livestreamed via a partnership with live music streaming platform Mandolin.

Additional announcements about programming are expected in the coming weeks.
