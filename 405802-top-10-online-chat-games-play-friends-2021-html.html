Top 10 online chat games you should play with friends in 2021

With the ever-growing technology, online chat games have become the best way of whiling time with friends. As a result, many exciting games have been developed to meet the insatiable appetite for playing. So, which ones do you know?

Most online chat games are simple and straightforward. Some are technical and are meant to gauge your mental capabilities. Nonetheless, they are all engaging and fun. For most of these games, no download is required. Therefore, familiarise yourself with them, and you are good to go.

10 online chat games

If you want a fantastic dose of nostalgic, fun texting games to pass time or looking for some cute emoji games, look no more. Here are some chat games for friends you can play on your phone or computer.

1. Story Builder

This is one of those real texting games you will undoubtedly enjoy. To play Story Builder, one of the players starts a story by texting a sentence to the other. The other person then texts the second sentence in the story back. Gradually, both participants build a story over the course of their engagement.

In some instances, players may decide to include additional rules. For example, players may decide to construct sentences containing a certain number of characters only.

2. 20 Questions

20 Questions is an old television show game that could also be played over the phone. To play twenty questions, one player must pick something; it could be an object, place or anything at all. Then their opponent will try to guess the object chosen in twenty questions or less.

There are rules applied in 20 Questions. They include;

  • An opponent can ask no more than twenty questions.
  • They have to be “Yes” or “No” questions.
  • Your answers should be a simple “Yes” or “No”. However, in some instances, you can answer using adverbs, such as “Sometimes,” usually,” or “rarely”.
  • You could also tell your opponent what category, your item falls, for example, an object, a place, a plant, among others.

4. Song Lyrics

When playing the Song Lyrics, one person quotes lines from a song to the opponent through a text. The opponent then has to guess the song the lyrics are from. It would be better if the game is played by people close to one another or share similar music tastes.

There are some variations though, which could include classic lines from movies or plots from plays. Failure to guess correctly could mean having to phone up and sing the song's chorus to your opponent.

5. Abbreviations

To play this one, you have to pick a category. Then mention an abbreviation or acronym in that category, and your friend has to tell you the correct meaning of the abbreviation. The person with the highest number of correct answers wins.

Some of the internet abbreviations may include;

  • OMG - Oh my god
  • LOL - Laughing out loud
  • TBH - To be honest
  • TTYL - Talk to you later
  • TL;DR - Too long; didn’t read

6. Virtual Team Builder

This is a fantastic game to play with your workmates and friends. There is utterly nothing better than an epic game when it comes to capacity building. Virtual Team Builder was designed to connect remote teams over video.

Their virtual team-building events could last for about 1-2 hours and 5 rounds that pack a fun punch. Every round avails a different engagement that brings employees together and keeps them laughing.

7. Emoji Challenge

This is one of the most exciting games to play on WhatsApp. One person sends an emoji, and the friends try to interpret the correct meaning behind it. It is such a fun thing to do. However, ensure you all the players have updated keyboards.

The chances are that if your keyboards are not updated, then what you send could be different from what the other person will get. In most cases, such a pastime would include something like merging a crown and lion emoji. This could suggest the Lion King movie.

8. Kiss, Marry, or Kill

This is one of the games to play over the phone with a friend. It is a popular and fascinating game to play with friends either in a personal WhatsApp chat or in a WhatsApp group chat. The players can ask other players who they would kiss, marry or kill from a choice of three people. These people can range from celebrities to friends or even people in the group.

9. Most Likely to Questions

This particular game lets you know what your friends and family think of you! It is a drinking game that takes you back to senior year of high school in some instances.

On your next WhatsApp chat, or text chat, or video call, take turns suggesting different "most likely to" scenarios, then have everyone vote on which one of you is to do that thing. Whoever gets more votes has to drink.

10. Questions Only

This texting game is a great way to pass time with friends. The rules are simple. All the participants can only ask each other questions—nothing else! When asked a question, you can only reply with another one and you cannot repeat a question that has already been asked. If someone takes too long to answer, they are out.

There you have it! These are some of the most interesting online chat games. However, there are many of them on the internet that you will be spoiled for choice. Next time you hang out with friends, family members, or colleagues, you could take advantage of such to pass the time productively.
