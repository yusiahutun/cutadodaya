Peaches Goes On Huge European tour

TORONTO (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Peaches has announced details of a massive European tour for the autumn NME reported.

The singer, who released new album 'Impeach My Bush' earlier this summer, will follow her appearance at the Carling Weekend: Reading and Leeds festivals with a host of dates across the continent, starting in Berlin on September 7.

The jaunt ends with three nights in the UK, including a London date at The Forum on October 13.

The full list of dates are as follows:

Carling Weekend: Reading Festival (August 25)
Carling Weekend: Leeds Festival (26)
Berlin Postbahhof (September 7)
