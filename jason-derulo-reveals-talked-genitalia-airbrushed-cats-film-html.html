Jason Derulo reveals his much-talked about genitalia was airbrushed out of Cats film

The much-anticipated music fantasy film Cats hits theaters on December 26. 

Musician Jason Derulo, 30, is among the star-studded cast who donned a form-fitting, and all-too revealing, costume during the shoot.

And on Monday, just over a week after making headlines for calling out Instagram for removing a picture of himself in a tight swimsuit, Derulo again found himself talking about his genitalia size with Andy Cohen on his SiriusXM radio show.

Confession: Jason Derulo claims his genitalia was airbrushed out of the Cats movie

During the conversation the Bravo host got candid and asked, 'You're in tights in Cats it makes me think they...' 

And in a moment's hesitancy, Derulo finished the sentence by adding, 'CGI'd the d*** out? Yeah, they did CGI [the penis out]. I noticed that.'

Cohen then joked with Derulo about him having to wear revealing tights on the set, while referencing his so-called anaconda swimsuit picture that was removed by Instagram.

'It was actually hilarious, [co star] Rebel Wilson was talking about it on and on on a show we were on. She was like, yeah man, we were all naked.'

During that guest appearance, Wilson all but confirmed the anaconda chatter by joking, 'Yeah I was around that for four months.'

After having his swimsuit picture removed, the Whatcha Say star shared a screenshot of the notice he got from the social media platform that claimed the content went against Instagram’s guidelines.

He captioned the new post: 'Fuk u mean? I have underwear on… I can't help my size..'
