Find Out How Kim Kardashian Persuaded Khloe to Shut Up Her Fat-Shamers

Play video content

Looks like Kim Kardashian is passing the "sexy" torch to sister Khloe Kardashian!

On the season 11 premiere of "Keeping up With The Kardashians," we see the moment Khlo first contemplated posing for her incredibly hot Complex magazine spread ... thanks to a little push from her older sis.

The star tells the soon to be mom-of-two that she's been getting a lot of comments lately about her weight loss. North West's mama suggests her sister do a sexy photo shoot to silence all of her haters once and for all.

"Someone said to me the other day, 'I looked at old pictures of you,' and they were like, 'You were so big!'" the blonde beauty reveals. "I was like,'Why are you looking at old pictures of me?' Guys and girls say this to me. It's like a known thing, how fat I was."

But the "Selfish" author, who's known for her love of gettin' naked, suggests that her sister take a page from her book.

"If you just do so many photo shoots it will replace all the bad ones," Kim advises. "Don't you want to replace every bad image of you?!"

Instagram

The star then drops a bombshell on an apprehensive Khloe, revealing that she got her the cover of Complex -- and Khloe doesn't exactly seem pleased.

"I just cannot believe Kim went as far as contacting the Complex people. I appreciate the effort but I am not like you Kim," she complains in a confessional. "I'm not as comfortable baring my naked ass on the cover of a magazine. It's just weird to do that."

Although she doesn't seem sold on the spread, we all know what happened next. The "Strong Looks Better Naked" author posed in a racy spread for the mag's August cover, where she flaunted her super fit bod in some very revealing workout attire.

This Sunday, we'll also get a look as Caitlyn Jenner prepares for her big night at the ESPYs and Kourtney Kardashian moving on from Scott Disick!
