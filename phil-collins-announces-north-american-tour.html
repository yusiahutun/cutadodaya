Phil Collins Announces North American Tour

(PRNewsfoto/Live Nation Entertainment)

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) Phil Collins has announced his first American concert tour in 12 years.

“Phil Collins Not Dead Yet, Live!” kicks off Oct. 5 at the BB&T Center in Ft. Lauderdale, Fla. And travels to the Forum in Inglewood, Calif., Oct. 28. In between it makes stops at places like Columbus, Cleveland and Chicago.

The show is expected to be a celebration of Collins’ many hits. He will be accompanied by longtime guitarist Daryl Stuermer, keyboardist Brad Cole, bassist Leland Sklar, percussionist Luis Conte and his 16 year old son Nicolas on drums along with a horn section and backup singers.

The tour is presented by Live Nation & S2BN Entertainment Studios by Arrangement with Solo & Tony Smith Personal Management.
