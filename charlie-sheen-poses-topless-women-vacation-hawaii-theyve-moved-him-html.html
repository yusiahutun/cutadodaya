He may be a father-of-five, but it's clear Charlie Sheen's idea of a good vacation is NOT family friendly.

The actor posed with three topless women on a recent trip to Hawaii.

The blonde, brunette and redhead grinned for a group shot with the actor in just their bikini bottoms.

He's at it again! Charlie Sheen poses with three new girlfriends (L-R) Jayme Langford, Jana Jordan and Celeste Star, on a recent trip to Hawaii

Sheen stood with his arms draped around his harem in front of an idyllic ocean scene, looking utterly delighted with his choice of holiday companions.

The foursome later reclined next to an infinity pool on sunloungers, before deigning to put on some clothes before their boarded Sheen's private jet.

And it appears the fun did not end there.

Sheen is now reportedly living with the trio of porn stars, including ginger haired Jayme Langford, blonde Jana Jordan and brunette Celeste Star.

Idyllic scene: The harem join Charlie on sun loungers next to an infinity pool

According to TMZ the three 'adult stars' have moved into Sheen's luxury home in Mullholland estates.

However Sheen denies the move, taking to his Twitter page to write: 'Contrary to all the silly rumors, these are the only girls I'm living with, who's got it better than me?'

And he posted an adorable picture of himself with daughters Sam and Lola

Despite his attempt to take the moral high ground, this would not be the first time Sheen has moved a 'harem' of girlfriends into his home.

If the reports were true it would be a throwback to 2011, when Sheen earned notoriety by moving in Bree Olson and Natalie Kenly, whom he dubbed 'the Goddesses'.

Traveling in style: The group pose outside Sheen's private jet, wearing Hawaiian Lei garlands around their neck

The Anger Management star was going through a public meltdown at the time, and his antics proved too much even for his open-minded girlfriends, who eventually moved out of their own accord.

His new arrangement comes as Sheen seemed to have moved into a more settled time.

His new TV show has won 26 Emmy nominations, much to star Sheen's credit.

And his family life also seems calmer.

The actor's former wife Denise Richards has been taking care of his four-year-old twins after his ex-wife Brooke Mueller lost custody in May due to her drug use. 

Along with her own two daughters with Sheen, and her adopted daughter, busy Denise has a house full.

And Sheen recently became a grandfather after his daughter Cassandra gave birth to a daughter Luna just last month. 

He certainly seemed pleased with the development, tweeting: 'hey Luna, welcome to my planet! #GrampaHare'
