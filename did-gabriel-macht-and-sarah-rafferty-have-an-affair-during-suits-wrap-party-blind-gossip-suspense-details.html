Did Gabriel Macht And Sarah Rafferty Have An Affair During Suits Wrap Party? Blind Gossip Suspense D

Gabriel Macht and Sarah Rafferty are having an affair are what people think if they watch the actors closely. But the reality is a little bit different.

Gabriel Macht and Sarah Rafferty are well-known actors known for their work in the Netflix series ‘Suits’.

The actor-duo played the leading role in the acclaimed series since the beginning and their on-screen and off-screen presence together has always raised a question among the fans.

Are Gabriel Macht and Sarah Rafferty are having an affair or they are just friends comfortable with each other and acting like one.

Well, let’s find out in an article.

Blind Gossip: Gabriel Macht And Sarah Rafferty Affair Rumor

Gabriel Macht and Sarah Rafferty’s affair rumor had hit its peak with all the blind gossip going around.

Macht and Rafferty, who started their journey as actors nearly 2 decades apart went to share their screen space together in the 2011 legal drama series ‘Suits’.

Their portrayal as Harvey Specter and Donna Paulsen caught the eyes of the fans even after not being portrayed as a couple, the reason being their off-screen chemistry.

Fans quickly turned their eyes into the actors as they went on to appear in the media together in such a close and comfortable nature which made the fans gossip around if they are having an affair.

The rumors became stronger when they locked their lips on the mid-season finale making fans believe that they are actually having an affair.

But the gossip could not last long as they revealed their relationship on the media. Let’s find out about their relationship on our next topic.

Gabriel Macht And Sarah Rafferty Relationship Explored

Gabriel Macht and Sarah Rafferty’s relationship as best friends have made fans surprised, given their on-screen presence together.

Having been best friends for over 20 years, Macht and Rafferty have found their relationship in a safe place as they have confirmed to have been together through thick and thin. They met for the first time at Williamstown Theatre Festival.

Rafferty’s professional journey was supported by her co-actor when she was new to the industry. Furthermore, Macht’s parents have been helpful to her career as well which had helped her land a role in the drama series.

Rafferty had revealed that Gabriel was a bridesmaid for her wedding ceremony and they care for each other like brother and sister.

Also, their children are best friends as well.

Gabriel Macht And Sarah Rafferty Partners 2022

Gabriel Macht and Sarah Rafferty are married to their respective partners.

Gabriel tied the knot with his wife Jacinda Barrett in 2004 and they share 2 children together.

Likewise, Sarah Rafferty exchanged her vows with Aleksanteri Olli-Pekka Seppala in 2001 and the couple has given birth to their 2 daughters Oona Gray and Iris Friday.
