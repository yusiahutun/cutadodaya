The sporty toppers became a fashion statement for both men and women in the 1950s and 1960s.

The 'Mama Said Knock You Out' singer relied on a hat to complete his undeniably cool outfits both on stage and off.

There are many ways to make a baseball cap look stylish

Gigi Hadid looked tres chic outside the Miu Miu show in a coordinated white getup and hat, with pops of purple

Here are some tips on how to incorporate baseball caps into your street style look:

Dress up your cap: Baseball caps can be dressed up by pairing them with more formal pieces such as a duster coat, maxi dress or dress pants. This juxtaposition creates an interesting contrast.

Go monochrome: For a sleek look, opt for a monochrome outfit and pair it with a matching hat, or a hat in a contrasting color.

Play with patterns: Team complementary patterns like a floral dress with a striped hat, or opt for head-to-toe coordination.

Experiment with colors: Pair a hat in a trendy color (think: neon or Barbie pink) with a neutral outfit, or mix and match with other bold colors for a playful, eye-catching look.
