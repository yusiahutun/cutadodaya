30 short professional bio examples on yourself that will get you noticed

Professional bios allow you to go into more detail than you will on your social media bios. However, coming up with a suitable bio for your profile could be challenging for most people. So, what are excellent short professional bio examples on yourself that will get you noticed?

A bio is a short paragraph in which you introduce yourself. Usually, people place bios on the "About Us" page of their professional website and social media pages. The ultimate goal is to provide an insight into who you are. Here’s everything to know when writing a short bio that would suit your website or brand.

How to write a professional profile about yourself

Your bio is often the first thing a potential employer, client or contact will read before contacting you. Therefore, your bio must be accurate, informative and memorable. You need to include the following in your bio:

You could decide to write a biography about yourself either in the first person or third person voice. Below are creative bio examples.

First-person bio template

My name is {your name}, and I'm passionate about {interest} and {interest}. I'm currently working as a {your current job title} where I practise {relevant skill} and {relevant skill} every day.

Third-person bio template

{Your name} works as a {Your current job title} for {your company}, where they regularly practise their {skill} and {skill}. With extensive experience in {career field}, they're passionate about {interest} and {interest}.
