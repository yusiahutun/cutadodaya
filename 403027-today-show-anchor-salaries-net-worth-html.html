Today show anchor salaries and net worth: how much do they make?

The Today show is an American award-winning morning TV show that airs on the National Broadcasting Company (NBC) network. The first episode of the show premiered on 14th January 1952. Dave Garroway was the host of the show during the time. The show has since evolved to become one of the most cherished and reliable morning shows in the U.S. It has competent anchors who always give in their best to ensure viewers are informed on various matters every morning. Can you guess what the Today show anchor salaries and net worth are in 2021?

The Today show cast comprises a team of journalists with top-notch skills from various fields. They are well-versed in politics, business, entertainment, and sports. Perhaps, this helps to explain why many viewers count on them for both domestic and international news. Apart from awards, many would love to know how much they make working on the show.

Today show anchor salaries and net worth

How much do the anchors on the Today show make? No doubt, they are among the highest-paid journalists in the industry. Their flashy lifestyles tell it all.

For instance, most of them own expensive properties and drive the latest luxury cars that match their class. Below is what each of them earns on the show and their net worth:

1. Savannah Guthrie

Savannah Guthrie is not a new name in the world of television broadcasting. Guthrie is an American professional journalist and licensed attorney. She joined NBC in 2008, working as the White House correspondent for a program titled NBC News. The 49-year-old worked on the show for approximately three years. She has since become the chief legal correspondent for NBC News and a co-anchor on Today.

Guthrie joined the Today show cast in 2011. She has since interviewed many high profile individuals in the government and celebrities. Some of the famous people she has interviewed include Barrack Obama, Donald Trump, Hillary Clinton, Gabby Giffords, Selena Gomez, Ashton Carter, Mark Zuckerberg, and Robert O’Neill.

2. Hoda Kotb

Hoda Kotb is an American-born television personality, broadcast journalist, and an established author. The Emmy-award winning journalist joined NBC in 1998, serving as a correspondent for Dateline NBC. Since joining the cast of Today, Kotb has covered many domestic and international events and breaking news.

Currently, she hosts Today alongside Savannah Guthrie. She is also a co-host of Today’s 4th hour. Some of the stories she has covered include Hurricane Katrina, War in Iraq, War on Terror in Afghanistan, 2012 Summer Olympics in London, and the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio.
