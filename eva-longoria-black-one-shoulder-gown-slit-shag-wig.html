Eva Longoria Captivates in a Black One-Shoulder Gown With a High Leg Slit

Not even a week into 2024 and Eva Longoria is already on a bombshell award show look streak. In her latest, she rocked a new hairstyle and stunned in a gown that was equal parts elegant and sexy.

Araya Doheny

The multi-hyphenate attended the La Cena Los Angeles at NeueHouse Hollywood on Friday night to celebrate Latino creatives in film and television. For the occasion, she traded her luscious long locks for a wet hair shag with bangs, which she revealed to People was a wig.

“It's not even my hair, but isn't it great?” she said. “Isn't that crazy? Let me tell you, it's award season—people are stepping it up.”

And, step it up, she did. To complement her striking new ‘do, Longoria captivated in a breathtaking black one-shoulder gown, which featured a heart-racing slit that soared to her hip and a sweeping, flowy construction. She let the centerpiece shine, keeping her jewelry limited to a couple rings, and pairing it with strappy black pumps. Longoria finished off her look, which was styled by Maeve Reilly, with a sultry dark smokey eye and matte peachy lip.

Rodin Eckenroth

Longoria spoke about her directorial debut Flamin’ Hot, which tells the story of Frito-Lay janitor Richard Montañez, who is the son of a Mexican immigrant, coming up with the idea for Flamin’ Hot Cheetos. The spicy snack, inspired by the flavors of his heritage, disrupted the American food industry.

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin

The night before, the actor-director swapped edginess for dreaminess at the Palm Springs International Film Festival. Longoria looked gorgeous in a lovely sheer lace hourglass dress embellished with stringy white fabric all over. She wore the sleeve-less, mock neck frock, which comes from Ashi Studio’s Fall/Winter 2023 couture collection, over a nude bodysuit and paired it with silky white platform heels and large rectangular diamond stud earrings.
