Meaning of Keep Save (Murder Rate) by Juice WRLD

In the world of music, the artist Juice WRLD has undoubtedly made a significant impact. Known for his emotional and introspective lyrics, Juice WRLD's songs often delve into personal struggles, heartbreak, and mental health. Among his vast discography, the song "Keep Save (Murder Rate)" stands out as a compelling exploration of various themes. This article aims to dissect the intricacies of the song, highlighting its themes and examining how each line contributes to the overall narrative.The overarching theme of "Keep Save (Murder Rate)" is the pursuit of escape and salvation from the troubles and darkness that surround the protagonist. From the opening lines of the song, Juice WRLD describes seeking solace and refuge from the murder rate, metaphorically representing the chaos and violence in the world. These lyrics shed light on the artist's desire to rise above the negativity and find a sense of calm and clarity. This theme resonates with many listeners who can relate to the yearning for tranquility and the feeling of being overwhelmed by the harsh realities of life.One standout lyric that delves into this overarching theme is: "Shut the fuck up, I can't concentrate, huh, shit, yeah." While these words might initially seem aggressive, they symbolize the struggle to find focus and inner peace amidst outside distractions. This theme of concentration aligns with the importance of maintaining clarity and keeping a level head in the face of adversity. It speaks to the idea that remaining calm and collected allows for better decision-making and a more positive outlook on life.Another impactful line from the song that contributes to the overall theme is: "On my knees, I been praying for brighter days." Here, Juice WRLD emphasizes the power of faith and spiritual introspection as a means to overcome challenges. The act of praying demonstrates a deep-rooted belief in something greater than oneself, offering hope and optimism in the midst of darkness. This theme of seeking brighter days resonates with the universal desire for a better tomorrow and serves as a reminder that there is always a glimmer of light even in the darkest of times.Delving deeper into the song, Juice WRLD's lyrics paint a picture of duality and transformation. In the line, "I got my gun, I'ma Mariah Carey it," the artist juxtaposes elements of danger with pop culture references. This unexpected combination highlights the multifaceted nature of the human experience, encapsulating moments of strength and vulnerability within one's personal journey. It suggests that individuals are capable of embracing their complexities and finding their own unique balance between light and darkness.Furthermore, the line "No one knew me, now bitches surrounding me" illuminates a theme of self-discovery and personal growth. It reflects the transformation from an individual who went unnoticed to someone who now commands attention and recognition. This theme resonates with those who have experienced significant changes in their lives and serves as a reminder that personal evolution is a constant process.One underlying theme that can be interpreted from the song is the concept of identity and authenticity. In the line, "Ain't no new me, I won't ever change, oh," Juice WRLD asserts his commitment to staying true to himself and retaining his individuality. This theme conveys the importance of embracing one's unique qualities and not succumbing to societal pressures or external influences. It encourages listeners to celebrate their individuality and find comfort in their own skin, despite the ever-changing world around them.The song "Keep Save (Murder Rate)" showcases the complexity of human emotions and the desire for redemption and liberation. Juice WRLD's introspective lyrics highlight the need for focus and concentration, the power of spirituality, the journey of self-discovery, and the importance of remaining true to oneself. These themes intertwine to create a compelling narrative that resonates with listeners who are grappling with their own personal struggles and aspirations. Ultimately, the song encourages individuals to persevere, seek solace within, and strive for a brighter future.
