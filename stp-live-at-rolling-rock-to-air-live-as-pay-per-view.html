STP, Live At Rolling Rock To Air Live As Pay-Per-View

The Stone Temple Pilots Live concert at Rolling Rock Town Fair 2.0 with the Deftones, Tantric and Oleander will be a two-hour rock music pay-per-view event on Saturday, Aug. 11 at 9 p.m. ET and 6 p.m. on Showtime Event Television (SET). The suggested retail price is $19.95. The Rolling Rock Town Fair 2.0 concert festival in Latrobe, Pa. is sold out.

Rolling Rock Town Fair has been created by Rolling Rock Beer. Rolling Rock has secured Lee Heiman of New York-based Track Entertainment as executive producer of Rolling Rock Town Fair 2.0.
