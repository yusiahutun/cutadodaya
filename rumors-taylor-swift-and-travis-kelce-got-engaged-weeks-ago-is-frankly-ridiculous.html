Rumors Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce 'got engaged' weeks ago is frankly ridiculous

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce probably aren’t engaged yet, despite rumors which say they decided to take the next step on New Year’s Eve.

The claim was made by a celebrity gossip website and podcast that the pair took the next step in their relationship. The celebrity couple have been going from strength to strength recently, but they only publicly confirmed their relationship in September last year. Taylor Swift attended numerous NFL games – her beau plays for the Kansas City Chiefs – and since then they have packed on the PDA every chance they get! But their relationship isn’t probably at the ring-on-Taylor’s-finger level just yet.

Why we don’t think they are engaged

We think any rumors Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are engaged are pretty ridiculous.

Taylor, 34, has been in relationships for much longer than her current one, such as her six-year romance with Hollywood star Joe Alwyn. This makes us wonder if she would actually tie the knot with someone she has only been with publicly for four months.

Not to mention, the couple also aren’t yet living together yet – even if Ms Swift has had quite a few ex-lovers in her time – they seem to be taking it slow.

Furthermore, the idea Travis would get down on one knee without telling a soul is questionable. Both Taylor and Travis are extremely close with their families, and so for them to take such a big step in secret is something we can’t believe.

Both Taylor’s mom and dad work with her, and she has written songs for her mom, so a big personal event like getting engaged, and on a whim too, just doesn’t really make much sense to us.

Rumors of Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce being ‘engaged’ emerge

During an episode of the Duex U podcast, anonymous hosts discussed the latest rumor surrounding Traylor. A source informed them the two were set to tie the knot. The source claimed before they attended a private party where they locked lips to bring in 2024, Taylor and Travis decided they were going to get hitched.

One of the hosts said they “didn’t necessarily believe” Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce had become engaged. Nonetheless, the host shared what the source had told her.

She said: “Somebody tells me, ‘Pretty sure Taylor and Travis got engaged on 12/31. Did it just the two of them alone before they headed to that party where they were photographed.”

Taylor and Travis were seen at the party looking “happy.” But it was New Year’s Eve. We don’t think it was ‘we just got secretly engaged’ type of happy.

Lyric Sheree, who was in attendance, told Entertainment Tonight: “I view them as a fresh couple. So, it always gives good vibes when you’re starting out to date, and they look very happy. Taylor looked super happy.

“I didn’t feel any type of bad chemistry between those two. I feel like they’re a really cute couple, and they have good energy together.”

A psychic predicted their engagement

Earlier this month, a psychic spoke exclusively to Celebrity Tidbit and told us all about how he believes Travis and Taylor will become engaged sooner rather than later. Adam Norton believes the special moment for the music star and sportsman will happen this year, too.

“Travis and Taylor I see working really well! An engagement I absolutely see on the cards, and it’s not too far away either!” he explained. “I keep seeing the night sky so potentially they’ll get engaged during the evening or a ring picture will be taken and put on social media during the evening…”

He continued: “But their relationship I do feel will continue to blossom into 2024. August / September and November / December will be very significant to Taylor and Travis’ relationship.

“She’ll have a ring on her finger by the end of 2024, without a doubt. I see their relationship moving/blossoming very quickly.”

Celebrity Tidbit has gone to Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s reps for comment.
