Presenters Christina Aguilera, Maluma, Meryl Streep &amp; More Announced For Grammy Awards

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – As the anticipation builds for the 66th Annual Grammy Awards, a star-studded array of presenters has been added to the lineup, promising a night filled with music and entertainment. The event will take place live from the Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles on Sunday (February 4) at 8 p.m. ET and will be hosted by Trevor Noah.

The presenters set to grace the stage include renowned artists and personalities such as Christina Aguilera, Samara Joy, Lenny Kravitz, Maluma, Lionel Richie, Mark Ronson, Meryl Streep, Taylor Tomlinson, and Oprah Winfrey. This diverse group will contribute to the celebration of Music’s Biggest Night, adding their unique flair to the evening’s proceedings.

Before the telecast, music enthusiasts can tune in to the Grammy Awards Premiere Ceremony, broadcast live from the Peacock Theater at 12:30 p.m. PT. The Premiere Ceremony will also be available for streaming on live.GRAMMY.com, providing an exciting prelude to the main event.

Previously announced performers for the 66th Annual telecast include Burna Boy, Luke Combs, Billie Eilish, Billy Joel, Dua Lipa, Joni Mitchell, Olivia Rodrigo, Travis Scott, SZA, and U2. With additional performers yet to be revealed, the night promises a diverse and captivating showcase of musical talent.

For those unable to catch the live broadcast on the CBS Television Network, streaming options are available on Paramount+.* Subscribers with SHOWTIME will have access to the live feed of their local CBS affiliate on Paramount+, while Paramount+ Essential subscribers can enjoy on-demand content the day after the special airs in the U.S. only. The 66th Annual GRAMMY Awards promises to be a night filled with unforgettable moments, celebrating the best in the world of music.
