Do the actors from The Office get royalties?

The Office's writers, actors, producers and creators get royalties, but nothing beyond their SAG-AFTRA residuals.

The US law states that actors are eligible for residual checks called royalties if shows are syndicated, redistributed, released on DVD, sold to streaming services or used in any other way beyond what the actors were initially paid.
