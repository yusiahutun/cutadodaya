The least humid city in Florida and the lowest RH ever recorded

Florida is one of the most beautiful states to visit in the United States. The state has a lot to offer to tourists and residents. However, humidity can be an issue and a turnoff for some people. So, if you fall in this category, it may help to know the least humid city in Florida.

What is the least humid city in Florida? The state is known for its hot and humid climate. However, some cities have lower humidity levels than the rest of the state.

Least humid city in Florida

There are no places with no humidity in the state of Florida. However, many cities experience lower relative humidity compared to others. According to peakng.com, these are some of the least humid cities in Florida.

1. North Bay Village, FL - 72.32%

North Bay Village is a city located in Miami-Dade County, Florida. The city had a total population of 8,159 as of the 2020 census. It has a humidity level of 72.32%, making it one of the least humid cities in the state of Florida.
