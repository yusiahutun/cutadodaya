Miley Cyrus reveals new 'sad kitty' tattoo on inside of her lip

Miley Cyrus rolls a suspicious-looking cigarette as she reveals new 'sad kitty' tattoo on inside of her lip

Miley Cyrus set tongues wagging on the internet on Friday as a photo of the star rolling a suspicious-looking cigarette was posted on Instagram.

The snap originally shared by Flaming Lips frontman Wayne Coyne shows the 21-year-old seated inside of a recording studio in Tulsa, Oklahoma, as she packs a rolling paper.

But the Wrecking Ball singer was apparently in need of a bit more exhibitionism as she shared another snap on Instagram of the inside of her lower lip that now features a new brightly coloured tattoo.

Suspicious: Wayne Coyne posted a picture of Miley Cyrus on Friday with the caption 'Yup...... Recordin with Miley... High as f***'

The photo posted by Coyne was captioned: 'Yup...... Recordin with Miley... High as f***.... #flaminglips #theflaminglips #tulsa #lovemoneyparty'.

The former teen star wore a jumper in the photo as she used her right index finger to pack her rolling paper.

And while Cyrus' newest inking is tucked into a place that would never see the light of day on most people, it will most certainly be getting it's fair share of attention on the orally fixated star.

Ouch! The 21-year-old showed off a new tattoo on the inside of her lip last week

Interesting choice: The singer's lip inking is of a sad kitty

She captioned the selfie: '#sadkitty #f***yeahtulsa @waynewontpostpicsofnakedwomen @katyweaver #lovemoneyparty #freak'. 

In the social media snap, the singer wears bright red nail polish as she pulls her lower lip down to reveal the inking.

Sporting a stripy red and black jumper, the blue-eyed beauty looked pretty in her blonde bob.

Coyne has been making surprise appearances on Miley's Bangerz tour and it looks as if the two have become fast friends.

Under the needle: In November the star had her grandmother's portrait immortalised on her arm


The last time Miley showed off a new tattoo was in November, which featured an everlasting ode to her grandmother Loretta Finley's portrait tattooed on her arm.

In an accompanying caption, she wrote: 'Because I am her favorite & she is mine'.

Miley, who at last count had 18 etchings all over her body, once again enlisted the services of her favourite tattoo artist Kat Von D to do the special inking honouring her grandmother.

The LA Ink star specialises in black and grey portraits, and appeared to be the perfect choice to make Miley's granny come to life on her arm.

'Still just as beautiful': Miley was a nice girl as she tweeted a sweet birthday tribute to her 'Mammie'

Progress: Kat Von D shared the progress of Miley's tattoo on her own Instagram page

The We Can't Stop star chose to use a vintage portrait of her beloved grandmother when it appears she was around the same age as the songstress is now.

Kat Von D proudly shared snaps of the design on her own Instagram page, showing the progress of the tattoo from start to finish.

Miley also posted an image of herself laid down on Kat's tattoo table as the famous artist got to work in her own private studio.

Surprise appearances: Wayne Coyne of the Flaming Lips has been popping up on Miley's Bangerz tour

Miley has become quite the regular at Kat's West Hollywood High Voltage parlor. 

She was last spotted leaving there back in August with a black bandage taped to her arm.

'@thekatvond is so ILL,' she wrote alongside a snapshot of the pair, while Kat wrote 'tattoo time,' on her own Twitter page.

Miley got her first tattoo at just 17-years-old when she had the words 'Just Breathe' inked below her ribcage.

Collection: Miley's tattoo collection features a dream catcher on her ribs, an inking of a quote from Theodore Roosevelt on her other forearm, a horn and 'love' written on the inside of her ear

She also has several small inkings including 'love' written on the inside of her ear, a heart, a cross and peace sign on her fingers and an equals sign signalling her support for gay marriage.

She said of the tattoo last year: 'When I shared a picture of my tattoo on my Twitter page and said, “All LOVE is equal,” a lot of people mocked me - they said, “What happened to you? You used to be a Christian girl!”'

And I said, “Well, if you were a true Christian, you would have your facts straight. Christianity is about love.”'

Outrageous: It remains to be seen what Miley's grandmother thinks of her granddaughter's recent wild antics

Miley also has a large dream catcher on her side, a skull on her ankle and the word 'karma' on one finger.

Kat Von D tattooed a mini replica of da Vinci's anatomical heart drawing on her forearm back in March this year and she also has an inking of a quote from Theodore Roosevelt on her other forearm.

The quote reads: 'So that his place shall never be with those cold and timid souls who neither know victory nor defeat'.
