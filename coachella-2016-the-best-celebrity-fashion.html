Coachella 2016: The best celebrity fashion

    Ellie Goulding mixed up her style in bold gold shorts and a Hunter Original Women's Colour Haze jacket in grey and orange.

    Chanel Iman showed off her toned stomach in a loose white crop top and laced up jeans.

    Kylie Jenner rocked an orange wig and captioned her Instagram post: "Crashing @simihaze set."

    Kendall Jenner rocked the boho looked with her long brunette hair pulled up her hair into two buns.

    Taylor Swift dazzled with bright blonde hair, aptly captioning her post: "bleachella."

    Suki Waterhouse looked casual in dungarees and a black bomber jacket and slides by Hunter, the British heritage brand.

    Vanessa Hudgens is Queen of Coachella, and had her nails done with Swarovski crystals especially for the festival. She captioned her photo: "Did someone say Coachella? Nails."

    Ruby Rose wore her hair blue-green braids and captioned her snap: "FAMchella."

    Paris Hilton looked both casual and elegant in a revealing lacy black dress and sunglasses.

    Brooklyn Beckham captioned his Instagram photo: "Coachella here we come."

    Fashion model Hailey Baldwin posed in a crop top and ripped jeans in the sunshine, captioning her photo: "First day of Coachella #hmlovescoachella"

    Jamie Chung wrote: "Copper chopper, get down! Lol Coachella ella here we come... BEST WAY to beat the traffic."

    Supermodel Sara Sampaio simply wrote: "all white" as she shared stunning photos of her chic outfit.

    Taylor Swift captioned her photo: "I feel like maybe chokers are the new flower crowns?"

    Brooklyn Beckham wore his hair in braids for the second day of Coachella.

    Kylie Jenner looked incredible in rainbow braids and a bikini with denim shorts.
