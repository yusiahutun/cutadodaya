Space NK advent calendar | Shop the 2023 beauty calendar now

It might only be the start of Autumn, but this year's beauty advent calendars are already hitting the shelves (before swiftly selling out). While pretty-much every retailer has one of their own these days, Space NK’s weighty offering always demands attention – after all, the retailer has been our go-to for iconic skincare, premium make-up and bestselling body products for years.

Space NK was relatively late to the advent-calendar craze, launching its debut offering, the Beauty Anthology, in 2019. However, the retailer certainly didn’t do things by halves: this four-tiered tome was packed with prestige products to see you through the late nights and cold mornings after with ease.

This year's Space NK Advent Calendar is the biggest one yet, boasting a remarkable 33 gifts, 19 of which are full sized. Worth over £1000 in total, it's full to the brim with beauty essentials and luxury treats that you'll actually use to the last drop, with big names sitting alongside new heroes.

It's safe to say that the contents of this year's Space NK calendar are impressive – in fact, we think this will be one of the best calendars of 2023. You can expect to find some of Space NK's most-wanted brands, including Augustinus Bader, Summer Fridays and Hourglass inside those brightly coloured boxes. For those who love discovering new heroes, expect cult bodycare line Necessaire and beauty-editor favourite Tatcha, as well as Rosie Huntington-Whiteley's predictably chic make-up line, Rose Inc.

If you really can't wait to find out exactly what's in store, see the full list of contents below.
